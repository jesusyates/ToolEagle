## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stuck with a basic bio? Your first impression is costing you followers. Here's a proven framework to craft a bio that converts scrollers into engaged fans.

### Script beats
- Open with a strong identity statement that names your niche immediately.
- Hint at the specific value or entertainment you provide in 1-2 clear points.
- Inject personality with a relatable flaw, curiosity, or ongoing quest.
- Include a subtle directive pointing viewers to your best content or a call to engage.
