## Curiosity-driven captions

These captions create a gap that viewers want to fill:

1. "You need to see this 👇"
2. "Nobody is talking about this…"
3. "POV: you're about to learn something"
4. "Stop scrolling for a sec 👇"
5. "I tried this so you don't have to"

## Short punchy captions

Keep it under 10 words for maximum impact:

1. "This changed everything."
2. "No one talks about this."
3. "Save this for later."
4. "The truth about [topic]."
5. "POV: [relatable moment]"

## Question captions

Questions invite comments and boost engagement:

1. "Would you try this?"
2. "Am I the only one who…?"
3. "What would you do?"
4. "Why doesn't anyone talk about this?"
5. "Agree or disagree?"

Match your caption to trending sounds and formats:

1. "POV: [trending scenario]"
2. "When [relatable situation]"
3. "Me when [funny moment]"
4. "That one friend who [relatable trait]"
5. "Plot twist: [unexpected ending]"
