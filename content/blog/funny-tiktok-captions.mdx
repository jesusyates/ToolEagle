## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that match your funny videos? These 50 proven formulas are designed to make your content instantly more relatable and shareable.

### Script beats
- Identify the core relatable moment in your video (e.g., awkwardness, procrastination, pet antics).
- Match the moment to a caption formula that exaggerates or names the feeling.
- Read it aloud to ensure it sounds natural and matches your comedic tone.
- Pair the caption with a fitting visual or text-on-screen for emphasis.
- Use the hashtag strategy to place it in front of laughing audiences.
