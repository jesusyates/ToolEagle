### Hook
Struggling to write captions that actually drive business results? This proven framework turns your posts into conversion tools.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable problem your ideal customer faces.
- Introduce your product or service as the specific solution.
- Highlight a key feature or benefit with tangible details.
- Address a potential objection or showcase social proof subtly.
- Direct the reader's next step with a clear, low-friction call-to-action.
