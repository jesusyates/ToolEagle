## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your GRWM is more than a routine; it's a story waiting for the right caption.

### Script beats
- Open with a specific emotional or situational context for the GRWM.
- Show a key product or step with a niche reason for its use.
- Share a quick personal insight or lesson linked to the routine.
- Pose a reflective question to the viewer about their own habits.
- End with the transformed feeling or mindset after getting ready.

### Caption / title
These captions transform simple 'get ready with me' clips into relatable micro-stories. Each formula is built for niches like burnout recovery, creative work preparation, or managing specific life seasons, providing the concrete detail that sparks comments.

### CTA
Which of these GRWM situations feels most real for you this week? Drop a '1' for morning routines or '2' for evening wind-downs in the comments.

### Why it works
- Moves beyond generic 'getting ready' to attach an emotional or situational hook.
- Uses specific, searchable niche details that attract targeted followers.
- The CTA is low-effort but highly engaging, prompting quick community interaction.
- Frames the GRWM as a relatable story, not just a procedural video.
