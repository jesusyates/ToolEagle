## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop Scrolling. Your next OOTD caption is here.

### Script beats
- Start with a specific, relatable styling pain point or goal.
- Reveal the core item or formula that solved it.
- Add a niche detail about sourcing, styling, or fabric.
- Hint at the benefit or feeling the outfit provides.
- Pivot to invite the audience into your styling process.

### Caption / title
These caption formulas break down the 'why' behind an outfit, moving beyond just listing items to share actionable styling insight that resonates with a fashion-focused audience.

### CTA
Which of these formulas will you try first? Comment '1' for workwear or '2' for weekend style!

### Hashtags
#OOTD #OOTDInspiration #StyleTips #FashionBlogger #OutfitDetails #InstaFashion #WhatIWore #FashionFormula #StyleCommunity #WardrobeEssentials #OutfitRepeater #SustainableFashion

### Why it works
- Moves beyond simple item lists to provide valuable styling insight.
- Uses specific details (like 'thrifting' or 'capsule wardrobe') to attract a niche audience.
- Structured to invite conversation by asking for preferences or experiences.
- Focuses on the benefit (confidence, comfort, versatility) which is more relatable than just aesthetics.
