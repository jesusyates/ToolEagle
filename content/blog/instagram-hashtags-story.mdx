## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get your stories seen? These hashtag collections target audiences who crave narrative.

### Script beats
- Identify the core narrative theme of your post
- Select 2-3 primary hashtags from relevant sets above
- Mix in 3-5 broader storytelling hashtags for reach
- Add 2-3 ultra-niche tags specific to your story's topic
- Avoid overused generic tags that drown specific narratives
