## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop what you're doing. Your first three seconds are wrong.

### Script beats
- State a common problem or surprising fact.
- Show the visual consequence or setup.
- Quickly demonstrate the solution or reveal.
- Hint at the full value in the caption.
- End with a direct question or transition.

### Caption / title
You're losing viewers in the first 3 seconds. Here's how to fix it.

### CTA
Which hook will you test first? Try it and tag us!

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Targets a specific pain point or curiosity gap.
- Uses 'you' language for direct audience connection.
- Promises a quick, tangible takeaway or revelation.
- Structure is optimized for Instagram's fast-paced feed.
