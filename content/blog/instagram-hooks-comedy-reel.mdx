## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
My therapist said I should try expressing my feelings more.

### Script beats
- Start with a confessional-style hook to camera.
- Cut to exaggerated visual metaphors for 'expressing feelings'.
- End with a punchline where the 'expression' backfires comically.
- Use quick cuts and reactive zooms for emphasis.

### Caption / title
Trying out my therapist's advice. It's going great.

### CTA
Which feeling should I express next?

### Hashtags
#comedyreel #instagramcomedy #funnyvideos #comedyskits #relatablecomedy #hookideas #contentcreator #reelideas #videohooks #comedian #instareels #viralfunny #contenttips

### Why it works
- Relatable premise creates immediate connection.
- Open-ended hook invites curiosity about the 'expression'.
- Visual comedy potential is high and platform-friendly.
- Confessional tone feels authentic and shareable.
