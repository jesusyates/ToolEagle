## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get views on your mom content? These 30 hooks are engineered to stop the scroll by speaking directly to the shared, real experiences of motherhood.

### Script beats
- Start with a relatable, specific mom moment or frustration.
- Add a twist, a lesson learned, or a simple solution.
- End with an open loop or a question that prompts a 'me too' in the comments.

### Caption / title
30 Mom Life Hooks to Boost Your Instagram Engagement

### CTA
Which one will you try first? Save this for your next content day!

### Hashtags
#MomLife #MomHacks #ParentingTips #Motherhood #MomBlogger #InstagramMoms #MomContent #ParentingHumor #MomTruth #ToddlerLife #MomCommunity #RealMotherhood #Mompreneur #SAHMLife

### Why it works
- Taps into highly specific, shared emotional experiences (frustration, joy, guilt).
- Uses curiosity gaps by hinting at a lesson or secret without giving it all away.
- Relatable and niche-aware, speaking directly to a mom's daily reality.
