## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
What if your quiet neighborhood held a secret that rewrote history?

### Script beats
- Open on a shot of an ordinary suburban street at dusk.
- Zoom in on a single, seemingly normal house with one odd detail.
- Reveal a cryptic clue found by the homeowner.
- Pose the core question that challenges reality.
- End with a direct, curious look at the camera.

### Caption / title
The quietest street in town has one rule everyone follows but no one questions.

### CTA
What's the weirdest rule in YOUR neighborhood? Share below.

### Hashtags
#MysteryReels UnsolvedMystery CreepyInstagram UrbanLegend Storytime PlotTwist CuriosityHook MindBlown WhatsTheTruth ViralMystery InstagramThriller Unexplained HookIdeas ShortFormStory

### Why it works
- Presents an immediate, relatable anomaly in a normal setting.
- Invites the audience to solve the puzzle, driving comments and shares.
- Uses visual storytelling that is perfect for Instagram's short-form video.
