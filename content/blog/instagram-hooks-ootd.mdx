## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
This outfit formula works for five different occasions in my week.

### Script beats
- Quick full-body outfit reveal with a confident stance.
- Close-up pan on the key versatile piece (e.g., blazer, shoes).
- Show quick cuts of you in different settings (office, cafe, errands).
- Text overlay highlighting the 'one piece, multiple ways' concept.
- End with a smiling direct-to-camera ask for outfit feedback.

### Caption / title
I built my entire week of outfits around this single blazer. It takes my look from morning meetings to casual dinners just by changing the accessories underneath. This is my secret weapon for looking put together with minimal effort.

### CTA
Comment 'FORMULA' if you want a full breakdown of how I style this piece, and save this for your next week of outfit planning!

### Hashtags
#OOTD #OutfitInspiration #StyleTips #FashionBlogger #InstaFashion #OutfitFormula #WardrobeEssentials #StyleHacks #FashionInfluencer #DailyLook #GetReadyWithMe #FashionReels

### Why it works
- Solves a common viewer problem (outfit repetition, versatility).
- Uses specific, relatable scenarios (office, dinner) for connection.
- Promises a tangible benefit (easy style, multiple looks).
- Encourages interaction with a simple, direct call-to-action.
