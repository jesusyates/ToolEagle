## The 3-tier hashtag system

**Tier 1 — Broad (1-2 hashtags):** #reels #instagram #viral
- High volume, high competition. Use sparingly.

**Tier 2 — Niche (5-8 hashtags):** #[yourniche] #[niche]tips #[niche]creator
- Medium volume, better reach. Your main focus.

**Tier 3 — Micro (3-5 hashtags):** #[very-specific] #[niche]forbeginners
- Low volume, high intent. Easier to rank.

## How many hashtags for Reels

1. **Instagram allows 30** — But you don't need that many
2. **10-15 is ideal** — Mix of tiers
3. **Rotate sets** — Don't use the same 15 every time
4. **Test and track** — See which sets drive discovery

<TipBox title="Pro tip">Put hashtags in the first comment if you want a cleaner caption. Both work for discovery.</TipBox>

## Hashtags to avoid

1. **Banned or restricted** — Check Instagram's guidelines
2. **Irrelevant** — Don't use #fitness on a food video
3. **Overused spam tags** — #like #follow #f4f
4. **Same set every time** — Rotate to avoid shadowban
