## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to name your delicious food content? These 30 proven titles are engineered for maximum saves and shares.

### Script beats
- Show the finished dish with a captivating visual.
- Quickly list the key ingredients or time commitment.
- Demonstrate one satisfying or tricky step in the process.
- End with the final plated result and a call to action.

### Why it works
- Titles promise a specific outcome or solve a common problem.
- They use power words like 'easy,' 'perfect,' and 'viral.'
- Each targets a niche interest within the broad food category.
