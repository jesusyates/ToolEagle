### Hook
Struggling to find a caption that matches your video's perfect aesthetic? Your caption is the final piece of the vibe.

### Script beats
- Open with a visually striking, slow-motion shot that establishes your core aesthetic (e.g., morning light, a tidy desk, a city walk).
- Cut to a closer, detailed shot focusing on a key textural element (hands on a keyboard, steam from a cup, turning a page).
- Use a quick text overlay or a smooth transition to reveal your chosen caption from this list on screen.
- End with a direct-to-camera smile or a final wide shot that embodies the caption's feeling, letting the music swell.
