## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that get your beauty videos seen?

### Script beats
- Setup: what this is about
- Proof: quick example
- Step: one actionable move
- CTA: ask for a comment/save

Get this sun-kissed glow using my favorite cream bronzer.

What's your go-to product for a natural glow? Tell me below!
