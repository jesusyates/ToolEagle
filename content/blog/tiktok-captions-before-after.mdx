## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop just showing the after. The magic is in contrasting it with the before.

### Script beats
- Show the relatable 'before' state visually.
- Reveal the improved 'after' result clearly.
- Briefly mention the key change or action taken.
- Prompt viewers to guess the method or share their own experience.

### Caption / title
You won't believe the difference this one change made.

### CTA
What's your best before/after transformation? Drop it below.

### Hashtags
#BeforeAfter #Transformation #LifeHack #DIY #Improvement #Results #TikTokTips #ContentCreator #HowTo #Process #Growth #Compare #Makeover #Upgrade

### Why it works
- Creates instant curiosity and a reason to watch fully.
- Makes your advice or method feel tangible and valuable.
- Encourages comments as viewers relate their own experiences.
