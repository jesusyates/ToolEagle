## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that get comments? Here are 50 proven formulas.

### Script beats
- Show a video with low engagement (sad music).
- Cut to you looking at the screen thoughtfully.
- Reveal a list on screen titled '50 Caption Formulas'.
- Quickly flash 3-4 example captions on screen with upbeat sound.
- End with you smiling, pointing to the caption area.

### Caption / title
I wasted months on bad captions. Use these 50 instead.

### CTA
Which caption formula will you try first? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#TikTokTips #ContentCreator #SocialMediaTips #TikTokForBeginners #CaptionIdeas #GrowOnTikTok #ContentCreation #DigitalMarketing #TikTokStrategy #EngagementTips #NewCreator #ViralTips #TikTokGrowth

### Why it works
- Directly addresses a common beginner pain point (low engagement).
- Provides immediate, tangible value with a high number of examples.
- The hook creates a relatable problem/solution dynamic.
