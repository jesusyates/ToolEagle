## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get comments on your TikToks? This caption framework turns viewers into participants.

### Script beats
- Open with a clear, simple challenge instruction.
- Show a quick example or demonstration of the challenge.
- React to the challenge attempt, emphasizing it's fun to try.
- Prompt viewers to share their own results in the comments.
- End with a direct call-to-action to participate now.

### Caption / title
I challenge you to try this and tag your attempt!

### CTA
Show me your version in the comments!

### Hashtags
#TikTokChallenge #CaptionChallenge #ViralChallenge #InteractiveContent #ContentCreator #EngagementTips #SocialMediaStrategy #CreatorEconomy #VideoMarketing #TrendAlert #AudienceGrowth #ContentIdeas #GoViral

### Why it works
- Challenges tap into competitive and social human psychology.
- Clear instructions lower the barrier for audience participation.
- User-generated content in comments boosts algorithmic ranking.
