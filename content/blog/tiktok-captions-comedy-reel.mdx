## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop posting funny videos with boring captions. Here's how to make the text work as hard as the visual gag.

### Script beats
- Identify the core relatable frustration in your clip.
- Phrase the caption as an internal monologue or confession.
- Keep it concise to match TikTok's fast-paced style.
- Use the caption to set up or underline the punchline.

### Caption / title
My brain on Monday morning is a special kind of chaos. #relatable #comedy

### CTA
Which one of these will you try in your next reel?

### Hashtags
#comedyreel #tiktokcomedy #funnyvideos #relatable #humor #skit #fyp #viral #foryou #comedian #everydaylife #lol #memes #contentcreator

### Why it works
- Relatable confession-style captions lower viewer defenses and increase shareability.
- They provide context that enhances the visual joke without explaining it.
- This format encourages comments as viewers tag friends who 'are like this'.
