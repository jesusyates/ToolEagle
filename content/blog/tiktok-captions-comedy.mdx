## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to find the right words for your hilarious TikTok skits? The caption is half the joke.

### Script beats
- Start with a relatable premise or observation.
- Build with exaggerated reactions or internal monologue.
- Punchline lands in the visual or a sudden realization.
- End on a beat that invites a duet or stitch.

### Caption / title
50 Comedy TikTok Captions for Skits, Rants & Relatable Humor

### CTA
Copy your favorite caption, paste it on your next video, and track the engagement difference.

### Hashtags
#ComedyTikTok #FunnyCaptions #RelatableHumor #TikTokSkits #ObservationalHumor #ViralComedy #ContentCreatorTips #TikTokStrategy #FunnyContent #CreatorTool #EngagementTips #VideoIdeas

### Why it works
- Captions provide essential context that makes the visual punchline land harder.
- They invite the viewer into a shared, relatable experience before the video even plays.
- Searchable and shareable text increases the chances of your content being discovered.
- A strong caption can frame the entire video, guiding the viewer's reaction.
