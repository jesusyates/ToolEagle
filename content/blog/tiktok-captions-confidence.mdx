## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to find the right words to match your confident vibe? This framework turns any clip into a statement of self-assurance.

### Script beats
- Open with a powerful, silent confidence moment (e.g., a steady look, adjusting your posture).
- Cut to a text overlay stating a core confidence belief from the caption list.
- Show a contrasting 'before' subtlety (like looking away) and the 'after' assurance.
- End on a simple, direct shot of you embodying that calm, sure energy.

### Caption / title
The confidence you project starts with the words you choose. #ConfidenceCaption

### CTA

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Focuses on internal state over external validation, aligning with authentic TikTok trends.
- Uses actionable, first-person language that creators can directly adopt and feel.
- Balances aspirational tone with achievable self-talk, avoiding unrealistic 'overnight' promises.
