## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get votes for your TikTok contest entry? Your caption might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Open with your contest entry video looking polished.
- Cut to a text overlay stating 'But my caption was weak...'
- Show a quick montage of low engagement metrics (likes/comments).
- Reveal a new, stronger caption from this list on screen.
- End with a call to action to use these captions for better results.

### Caption / title
I used these captions and my contest engagement tripled.

### CTA
Copy your favorite caption below and test it on your next contest entry.

### Why it works
- Captions directly prompt the specific actions (votes, shares) needed to win.
- They provide social proof and narrative, making viewers more invested in your success.
- Strategic framing can make your entry stand out in a crowded contest feed.
