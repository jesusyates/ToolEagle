## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get comments? Controversy creates conversation. Here's how to frame it.

### Script beats
- Start with a strong, clear statement of your unpopular opinion.
- Present one or two concise reasons backing your viewpoint.
- Pose a direct question to the viewer to invite their perspective.
- Use on-screen text or visuals to emphasize your key argument.
- End by acknowledging other viewpoints exist to keep it open.

### Caption / title
I know this is controversial, but... [State Your Opinion]. What do you think?

### CTA
Drop your take in the comments—I'm reading every one.

### Hashtags
#controversial unpopularopinion debate discussion tiktokthoughts hotake opinion viral fyp foryou comment engagement creator

### Why it works
- Taps into the human desire to compare and contrast viewpoints.
- The algorithm favors content that sparks high comment engagement.
- Builds a more invested community through shared (or opposed) values.
- Positions the creator as a thought-starter within their niche.

## Best for TikTok

1. Creators in lifestyle, opinion, or commentary niches
2. Accounts looking to boost comment engagement and debate
3. Building a community around strong, relatable viewpoints
