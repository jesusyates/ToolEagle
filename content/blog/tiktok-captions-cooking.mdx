## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that make people watch your cooking videos?

### Script beats
- Show the finished dish quickly in the first second.
- Demonstrate one key, surprising technique or ingredient swap.
- Close-up on the satisfying final result or first bite.

### Caption / title
You won't believe how easy this weeknight dinner actually is.

### CTA
Save this for your next pasta night and try it!

### Hashtags
#CookingHacks #EasyRecipes #FoodTok #HomeCook #KitchenTips #QuickMeals #RecipeIdeas #CookingTutorial #FoodContent #LearnToCook #DinnerIdeas #BudgetCooking

### Why it works
- Provides immediate, actionable value in the caption.
- Uses curiosity and specificity to stop the scroll.
- Aligns with TikTok's preference for quick, useful tips.
