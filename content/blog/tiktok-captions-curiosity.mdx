## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop viewers mid-scroll with captions that make them HAVE to know more.

### Script beats
- Open with a mysterious visual or unanswered question.
- Build tension by hinting at a revelation or secret.
- Deliver value but leave a thread for the comments.
- Use editing to emphasize the moment of discovery.
- End with a prompt that ties back to the initial curiosity.

### Caption / title
You won't believe what happens when you try this.

### CTA
Comment your guess before the reveal!

### Hashtags
#curiosity #tiktokcaptions #contentstrategy #viralhooks #engagementtips #stopthescroll #creatoradvice #algorithmhack #watchtime #commentchallenge #mindblown #didyouknow #learnontiktok #contentcreation

### Why it works
- Curiosity creates an 'information gap' the brain must close.
- Questions prompt automatic mental engagement from viewers.
- Mystery leverages the Zeigarnik effect (unfinished tasks linger in memory).
- This format directly boosts metrics that the algorithm rewards.
