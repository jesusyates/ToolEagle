## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Think dad content is basic? These caption formulas prove it's a goldmine.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, relatable dad clip (e.g., a dad joke, a DIY fail, a proud moment).
- On-screen text highlights the core dad trait in the video.
- Use a quick cut or zoom to emphasize the funny or heartfelt reaction.
- End on a warm or punchline moment that matches the caption's tone.

### Caption / title
Dad levels: expert. #DadLife

### CTA
Which dad trope did we miss? Drop yours below. 👇

### Hashtags
#DadLife #DadJokes #Fatherhood #DadAndMe #FamilyTikTok #Parenting #Relatable #DadHumor #GirlDad #BoyDad #Papa #Family #DadContent #DadGoals

### Why it works
- Taps into a universally understood and highly relatable character archetype.
- Blends humor with heart, encouraging both laughs and positive comments.
- The niche is broad enough for mass appeal but specific enough to build community.
