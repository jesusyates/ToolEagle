## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your dance reel stops the scroll, but does your caption start the conversation?

### Script beats
- Open with a question about the viewer's own dance journey.
- Share a specific technical or emotional detail from the routine.
- Hint at a follow-up video or deeper dive into the process.
- Pose a reflective question to spark comments about their experience.

### Caption / title
The story behind the steps is what makes people stay.

### CTA
Which move should I break down next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#DanceReel #DanceTikTok #Choreography #DanceLife #DanceCreator #LearnToDance #DanceRoutine #DanceVideo #DanceChallenge #DanceCommunity #DanceTips #BehindTheDance

### Why it works
- Invites viewers behind the curtain of the performance.
- Uses niche-specific terminology that resonates with dance audiences.
- Encourages interaction by asking for input on future content.
- Balances artistic expression with practical, shareable insight.
