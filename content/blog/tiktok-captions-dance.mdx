### Hook
Struggling to write captions that get your dance videos seen?

### Script beats
- Identify the core story or technique in your video.
- Select a caption that matches your video's intent (educational, emotional, hype).
- Pair the caption with relevant, high-traffic dance hashtags.
- Include a clear call-to-action to drive comments or shares.
- Analyze performance to see which caption styles work best.
