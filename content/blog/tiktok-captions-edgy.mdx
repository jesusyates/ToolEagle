## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Tired of soft, agreeable captions? Here's how to write with an edge that actually gets remembered.

### Script beats
- Define what 'edgy' means for your niche (it's not just being mean).
- Pair the edgy caption with contrasting visual tone (smiling, soft visuals).
- Use the caption to frame a relatable, slightly uncomfortable truth.
- Keep the delivery confident, not aggressive, in the voiceover.
- Let the caption be the punchline or the mic drop moment.

### Caption / title
Edgy isn't about shock value; it's about uncompromising authenticity.

### CTA
Which of these lines hits different? Drop your favorite number below.

### Hashtags
#EdgyTikTok #MindsetCaptions #AuthenticContent #NoFilter #ConfidenceBoost #ViralCaptions #TikTokStrategy #ContentCreatorTips #AttitudeCheck #RealTalk #FYPStrategy #CaptionIdeas #ViralHooks #CreatorLife

### Why it works
- Edgy captions create high engagement through strong agreement or disagreement.
- They help creators stand out in oversaturated, 'nice' content feeds.
- They attract a loyal, niche audience that values blunt authenticity.
