## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
The caption template I use to double my engagement. Steal it.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: 'The exact caption formula I used on this video (230K views).'
- Quick cuts showing 3-4 different viral videos with similar caption structure.
- Reveal the core formula: [Intrigue] + [Lesson] + [Question/CTA].
- Show a before/after example of a weak vs. strong caption.
- End with a call to use one of the 50 captions below.

### Caption / title
The caption framework behind my most viewed business TikToks. (Formula in comments) ↓

### CTA
Which number caption are you using first? Drop it below. 👇

### Hashtags
#entrepreneur #businessowner #startup #smallbusiness #businesstips #entrepreneurmindset #contentstrategy #tiktokforbusiness #digitalentrepreneur #solopreneur #marketingtips #businessgrowth #captionideas #viralcaption

### Why it works
- Provides immediate, tangible value (a swipeable formula) in the hook.
- The 'reveal' structure creates curiosity and keeps viewers watching.
- The CTA is low-effort (commenting a number) which boosts engagement signals.
- Hashtags blend broad business topics with specific TikTok growth tags.
