## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get comments on your event TikToks? These captions are designed to fix that.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, compelling clip from your event.
- On-screen text poses the caption question.
- End with a clear CTA to engage in comments.

### Caption / title
Which of these would get you to buy a ticket?

### CTA
Comment your answer below!

### Hashtags
#EventPlanning #EventMarketing #TikTokForBusiness #ConferenceTips #FestivalVibes #Meetup #EventProfs #SocialEvent #PartyPlanning #EventCreator #ExperienceDesign #GoViral

### Why it works
- Questions and prompts directly invite viewer participation.
- Niche-specific terms resonate with the event creator community.
- Clear CTAs reduce friction for comments and shares.
