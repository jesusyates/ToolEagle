## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
POV: You're trying to find a caption for your family TikTok that isn't just 'family time'.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, relatable slice of family life (e.g., kids arguing over a toy, a sleepy morning cuddle pile).
- Use text overlay to state a simple, universal family truth.
- Cut to a reaction shot (your smiling/weary face, a hug resolving the conflict).
- End with a warm, genuine smile to the camera or a shot of the peaceful aftermath.

### Caption / title
50 captions for your family content that actually get comments.

### CTA
Which caption will you try first? Save this list for later!

### Hashtags
#familytiktok #parenting #momlife #dadlife #family #parentinghumor #sahm #workingmom #toddlerlife #familytime #momtok #reallife #familycontent #parentingwin

### Why it works
- Focuses on emotion and shared experience over perfection, which drives connection.
- Captions are short, open-ended, and encourage viewers to share their own stories in the comments.
- Uses positive but realistic framing that avoids being overly saccharine, increasing relatability.
