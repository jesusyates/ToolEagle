## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to make your finance content stand out? Your caption is the key.

### Script beats
- Identify a common pain point your audience feels.
- Flip a misconception with a clear, actionable statement.
- End with a question or prompt to drive comments.

### Caption / title
Your caption should challenge a belief or reveal a hidden cost.

### CTA
Which money myth bothers you the most? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#FinanceTips #MoneyTok #Investing #PersonalFinance #Budgeting #FinancialFreedom #StockMarket #Savings #DebtFree #CreditScore #SideHustle #FIRE #InvestForBeginners #WealthBuilding

### Why it works
- Addresses immediate financial frustrations viewers recognize.
- Uses strong verbs to create a sense of urgency and clarity.
- Prompts engagement by asking for opinions or experiences.
