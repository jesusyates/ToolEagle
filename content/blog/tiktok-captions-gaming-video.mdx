## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using boring captions on your gaming clips.

### Script beats
- Use a caption that hints at the story or struggle.
- Mention a specific detail only players would notice.
- Frame it as a question to other gamers.
- Add a touch of humor or relatable frustration.
- Keep it concise and scroll-stopping.

### Caption / title
Gaming captions that make viewers want to comment.

### CTA
Copy your favorite caption and try it on your next video.

### Hashtags
#gamingtiktok #gamingclips #gamingsetup #pcgaming #consolegaming #indiegames #gamingcommunity #gamingmemes #gaminglife #gamer #gamergirl #fypgaming #videogames

### Why it works
- Speaks directly to a niche gaming community.
- Uses insider language that builds credibility.
- Encourages interaction through shared experience.
- Short enough for TikTok's fast-paced scroll.
