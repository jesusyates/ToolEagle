### Hook
Struggling to caption your gaming clips? Stop overthinking and use one of these 50 proven lines made for TikTok's vibe.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick, relatable gaming struggle (like controller drift or a failed jump).
- Show your gameplay clip that visually matches the caption's scenario.
- Add text overlay emphasizing the key emotion or action from the caption.
- Use a quick cut or zoom to highlight the funny or intense moment.
- End with you reacting genuinely—a laugh, a head shake, or a victory pose.
