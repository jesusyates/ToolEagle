## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your Get Ready With Me videos?

### Script beats
- Show your authentic morning or evening routine.
- Highlight a specific product or time-saving hack.
- Share a personal story or reason behind the routine.
- Ask viewers to share their own tips in the comments.
- End with a clear call-to-action for engagement.

### Caption / title
Get Ready With Me: [Your Specific Routine/Vibe]

### CTA
What's your can't-live-without product? Tell me below!

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #MakeupTutorial #SkincareRoutine #BeautyTok #TikTokBeauty #MorningRoutine #RoutineCheck #Makeup #BeautyHacks #SelfCare #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #ViralBeauty

### Why it works
- GRWM content is highly relatable and builds personal connection.
- Viewers love seeing authentic routines and discovering new products.
- The format encourages watch time and comment interaction.
