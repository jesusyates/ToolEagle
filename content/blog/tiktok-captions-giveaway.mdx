## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
You're giving away free stuff, but is anyone entering? These captions turn viewers into participants.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick visual of the prize
- State the simple entry requirements clearly
- Show genuine excitement about giving back to your community
- Create urgency with a clear deadline
- End with a strong call to action

### Caption / title
GIVEAWAY TIME: How to Enter & Win

### CTA
Enter now before time runs out!

### Hashtags
#giveaway #tiktokgiveaway #win #free #contest #enter #giveawaytime #mustfollow #fyp #viral #trending #prize #luckydraw #follow

### Why it works
- Clear instructions reduce friction for potential entrants
- Multiple entry methods can significantly boost overall engagement
- Urgency and excitement encourage immediate action from viewers
