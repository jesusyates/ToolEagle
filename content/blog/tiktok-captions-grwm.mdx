## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your Get Ready With Me TikToks?

### Script beats
- Introduce the purpose of your GRWM video briefly.
- Mention a key product or step that viewers might find interesting.
- End with a question or invitation to engage in the comments.

### Caption / title
GRWM: My [Time of Day/Routine Type] Routine Using [Product Type/Theme]

### CTA
What's your favorite product in your routine? Tell me below!

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #MakeupRoutine #SkincareRoutine #BeautyTikTok #MakeupTutorial #Routine #SelfCare #Makeup #Skincare #Beauty #GRWMTikTok #GRWMRoutine

### Why it works
- GRWM content is highly relatable and invites viewers into your personal space.
- Using specific details (like skin type or product price) attracts niche audiences.
- Ending with a question can boost comment engagement significantly.
