## Hacks Captions

1. Start with a question that demands a yes/no answer.
2. Use parentheses to add a quick, whispered aside.
3. Place your most compelling point before the "See more" line.
4. Add a one-word reaction in brackets for instant relatability.
5. Pose a controversial opinion to spark debate in comments.
6. Use "POV: You're..." to drop viewers into a scenario.
7. End with "Drop a [emoji] if..." for simple engagement.
8. Include a specific timestamp for a key moment.
9. Add "(Wait for it)" to build anticipation mid-caption.
10. Use ALL CAPS for a single, impactful word only.
11. Ask viewers to "Tag someone who needs this."
12. Start with "Okay, here's the tea on..." for gossipy hooks.
13. Use "Swipe up for part 2" even without the link.
14. Add "(Sound on!)" when audio is essential.
15. Pose two choices and ask "Which one are you?"

17. Add "(This changed everything)" to create urgency.
