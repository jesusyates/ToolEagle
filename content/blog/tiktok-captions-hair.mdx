## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that make people save your hair tutorials?

### Script beats
- Identify the specific hair problem your video solves
- Show the transformation or result in the first three seconds
- Explain the key product or technique in a simple step
- Use text overlays to emphasize the main benefit
- End with a question to prompt comments about their hair type

### Caption / title
Hair hack you probably haven't tried yet.

### CTA
Save this for your next wash day!

### Hashtags
#hairtok #hairhack #curlyhair #haircare #hairtutorial #hairgrowth #hairstyle #hairtips #diyhair #hairroutine #healthyhair #hairgoals #hairtransformation #hairlove

### Why it works
- Addresses a specific, common pain point (frizz, growth, damage)
- Uses concrete details (product names, times, techniques) that feel trustworthy
- Encourages saves by framing the tip as a future reference
