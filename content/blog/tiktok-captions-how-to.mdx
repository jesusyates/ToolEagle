## How To Captions

1. Start your caption with a question that sparks immediate curiosity.
2. Use a number to promise a specific, quick benefit in the first line.
3. Add a time frame to create urgency and encourage immediate watching.
4. Directly address a common pain point your niche audience experiences daily.
5. Hint at a surprising result or outcome revealed only in the video.
6. Frame the caption as a 'secret' or 'hack' your industry doesn't share.
7. Incorporate a trending audio reference that your target viewers will recognize.
8. Ask for a simple yes/no answer to lower the barrier for comment engagement.
9. Use parentheses to add a quick, relatable aside or internal thought.
10. Challenge a common misconception within your specific content niche.
11. Pose a 'this or that' style choice that is highly relevant to your topic.
12. Tease a before-and-after transformation that the video will demonstrate.
13. Start with 'POV:' to instantly frame a relatable scenario for the viewer.
14. Use an emoji to visually break up text and convey tone immediately.
15. Create a mini two-sentence story with a setup and a quick payoff.
16. Make a bold, slightly controversial statement to stop the scroll.
17. Offer a very specific tip that saves time or money for your audience.
18. Use all caps sparingly on one or two words for dramatic emphasis.
19. Ask viewers to 'double-tap if' they agree with a simple, common sentiment.
20. Share a quick lesson you learned from a recent, specific mistake.
21. Compare two approaches, labeling one as the method most people get wrong.
22. Give a single, actionable command like 'Save this for your next grocery trip.'
23. Mention a specific tool or product that solved a niche problem for you.
24. Use slang or jargon that your specific community understands and uses.
25. Start with 'Stop doing this...' to correct a widespread but minor error.
26. Frame the advice as something you wish you knew when you were starting.
27. Ask a 'why does nobody talk about...' question regarding a niche frustration.
28. Use a food or cooking analogy to explain a complex topic simply.
29. Hint that the video will show a faster way to complete a routine task.
30. Share a surprising statistic related to your niche in a conversational way.
31. Give permission to break a silly or outdated 'rule' in your industry.
32. Reveal a single ingredient or component that changed your entire process.
33. Tell viewers to listen with sound on for a key piece of audio information.
34. Use brackets to clarify intent, like [wait for the result] or [seriously].
35. Ask viewers to tag a friend who needs to see this specific piece of advice.
36. Promise to explain a concept in under ten seconds within the video.
37. Admit to a recent failure before showing the improved method in the video.
38. Use a carousel-style caption listing three quick tips without explanations.
39. Point out a subtle detail in the video that most people would miss.
40. Start with 'If you do [X], you need to see this.' for direct relevance.
41. Share a screenshot of a DM or comment that inspired the video's topic.
42. Use a seasonal or holiday hook to make the advice feel immediately timely.
43. Break down a professional term into simple, everyday language.
44. Give credit to another creator who inspired this specific tip or method.
45. Ask 'Am I the only one who...' to voice a shared, niche experience.
46. Use a progress bar emoji to visually represent a step-by-step process.
47. Mention a specific platform algorithm quirk and how to work with it.
48. Frame the video as a response to a frequently asked question you get.
49. End with 'Thoughts?' to explicitly invite opinions and discussion.
50. Combine two unrelated niches to create a novel, cross-disciplinary tip.
