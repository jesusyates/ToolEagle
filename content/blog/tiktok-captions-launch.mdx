## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your launch caption can make the difference between a scroll-past and a conversion.

### Script beats
- Start with a strong, emotion-driven opening line about the launch.
- Clearly state what is launching and its core promise or primary benefit.
- Create urgency with a time limit, limited quantity, or exclusive offer.
- Direct viewers to your link in bio or website for the next step.

### Caption / title
It's Launch Day! Here's Everything You Need to Know.

### CTA
Tap the link in bio to be among the first to get it.

### Hashtags
#LaunchDay #NewProduct #NowLive #AvailableNow #TikTokLaunch #ProductLaunch #ComingSoon #DroppingSoon #JustLaunched #CheckItOut #LimitedTime #FoundersEdition #GameChanger #ViralLaunch

### Why it works
- Builds anticipation and frames the launch as an event.
- Provides clear, actionable next steps for interested viewers.
- Uses urgency and exclusivity levers common in high-converting launches.
- Balances excitement with concrete details about the offer.
