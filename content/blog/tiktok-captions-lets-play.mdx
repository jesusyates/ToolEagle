## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to caption your Let's Play clips? These 50 phrases are engineered for the TikTok algorithm.

### Script beats
- Use a surprising or relatable moment from your gameplay as the visual hook.
- Pair it with a caption that adds context, humor, or emotion.
- End with a clear CTA to drive comments or shares.
- Keep the pacing fast to match platform expectations.
