## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling! I tested 100 life hack captions and these 3 formulas got 90% of my saves.

### Script beats
- Show the common problem visually (messy cables, stale food).
- Demonstrate the simple hack in under 3 seconds with clear steps.
- On-screen text highlights the key benefit (saves time, saves money, organizes).
- End with a CTA prompting a specific, low-effort engagement action.

### Caption / title
Life hacks that actually work (and the captions that make them viral).

### CTA
Which hack are you trying first? Save this for later!

### Hashtags
#LifeHacks #TikTokHacks DIY HomeHacks KitchenHacks Organization SaveMoney CleaningTips Productivity EasyTips ViralHacks SmartTips EverydayHacks FYP

### Why it works
- Captions frame the hack as a quick, tangible solution to a common annoyance.
- Direct questions in the CTA ('Which first?') prompt comments and increase watch time.
- Hashtags like #LifeHacks and #DIY target high-intent viewers actively searching for this content.
