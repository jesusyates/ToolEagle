## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that actually get engagement? Here are 50 proven ones.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable caption struggle.
- Show a quick montage of 3-5 high-engagement videos using these tactics.
- Explain that captions are conversation starters, not afterthoughts.
- Present the list as a swipe-worthy resource.
- End by encouraging them to save the video for later.

### Caption / title
50 TikTok Captions That Actually Get Comments & Shares (Save This!)

### CTA
Which number will you try first? Save this for your next video.

### Hashtags
#TikTokCaptions #ContentCreator #SocialMediaTips #TikTokStrategy #EngagementHacks #CaptionIdeas #GrowOnTikTok #ContentCreation #DigitalCreator #TikTokMarketing #ViralCaptions #CreatorEconomy #ShortFormContent #TikTokTips

### Why it works
- Each caption is an open-ended question or strong opinion designed to provoke a response.
- They are niche-aware but adaptable, allowing creators to insert their specific topic.
- The list format provides immediate, swipeable value, encouraging saves and shares.
