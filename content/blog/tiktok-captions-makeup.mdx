## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I tested 50 different caption formulas on my makeup TikToks. These 5 drove the most comments.

### Script beats
- Quick montage of 5 successful videos with high comment counts flashing on screen.
- Break down the first winning caption formula with a text overlay example.
- Show a B-roll clip of applying makeup while the second formula appears.
- Reveal the third formula, emphasizing its question-based structure.
- End with a call to action to try one of the 50 captions provided.
