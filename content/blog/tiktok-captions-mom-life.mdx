## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Ever stare at a blank caption box after filming the perfect mom-life moment? We've all been there.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, relatable mom moment (e.g., looking at camera while holding a cold coffee).
- Cut to a text overlay: 'Need a caption?'
- Rapid-fire scroll through 3-4 of the captions from the list on screen.
- End on a smiling, genuine shot of you with your kids or in your element.

### Caption / title
50 captions for when you're mom-tired and caption-dry. Save this! #MomTok

### CTA
Which number caption do you relate to most? Drop it below! 👇

### Hashtags
#MomLife #MomTok #Motherhood #Parenting #MomHacks #ToddlerLife #MomCaptions #MomBlogger #MomTruth #Parenthood #MomHumour #MommyAndMe #RealMotherhood #MomCommunity

### Why it works
- Solves a real, frequent pain point for creator moms (caption block).
- Provides immediate, tangible value (50 usable captions).
- Encourages engagement by asking viewers to identify with a specific number.
- Uses high-traffic, community-focused mom hashtags to boost discoverability.
