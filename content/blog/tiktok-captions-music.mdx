## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I used these caption formulas to get 10K+ comments on music TikToks.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: 'Caption Formula That Gets Comments'
- Quick cuts showing 3 different videos using captions from the list.
- Point to the screen where the caption appears on each video.
- Show the high comment counts on those videos (blurred usernames).
- Hold up a notepad or show a phone screen with the list title.

### Caption / title
Steal these music caption ideas for more comments & duets.

### CTA
Which number is your favorite? Save this for your next video.

### Hashtags
#musiccaption #tiktokcaption #contentcreator #musicmarketing #songwriter #musicdiscovery #captionideas #tiktoktips #musictok #contenttips #audienceengagement #musicvideo #creatortips #viralcaption

### Why it works
- Uses social proof ('10K+ comments') to establish immediate credibility.
- The 'steal these' language is low-friction and permission-giving for the viewer.
- The CTA ('Which number...') is an easy, direct question that prompts comments.
- Focuses on a tangible result (comments/duets) that every creator wants.
