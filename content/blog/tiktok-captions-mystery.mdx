## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write a caption that makes viewers NEED to know what happens next?

### Script beats
- Open with a cryptic visual or strange sound.
- Pair it with one of these captions that poses an unanswerable question.
- Let the mystery build in the comments as viewers theorize.
- Use the CTA to guide them to your next clue or video.

### Caption / title
You won't believe what they found in the walls.

### CTA
What's your theory? Comment below before Part 2 drops.

### Hashtags
#MysteryTok #UnsolvedMystery #CreepyTikTok #Storytime #Thriller #Suspense #PlotTwist #Unexplained #TrueCrimeTok #Paranormal #DarkAcademia #WritingPrompt #ConspiracyTheory #MindBlown

### Why it works
- Poses an immediate, intriguing question that demands an answer.
- Leverages the human instinct for pattern recognition and puzzle-solving.
- Encourages comment engagement as users debate and share theories.
- Creates perfect fodder for a multi-part series to boost retention.
