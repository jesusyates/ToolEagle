## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling. Your next favorite product just dropped.

### Script beats
- Quick cut of unboxing the new product
- Close-up shots highlighting key features
- Demonstration of the product in use
- Text overlay with main benefits
- Call to action to shop now

### Caption / title
New Arrival Alert: This Changes Everything

### CTA
Shop the new drop before your size sells out.

### Hashtags
#NewArrival #ProductLaunch #JustDropped #ShopNow #MustHave #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #NewProductAlert #ShoppingTikTok #SmallBusiness #Drops #LimitedEdition #RetailTherapy #NewCollection #TikTokShop

### Why it works
- Creates urgency with newness and limited availability
- Highlights product benefits through demonstration
- Uses popular shopping-related hashtags for discovery
- Direct call to action encourages immediate engagement
