## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to describe your outfit beyond 'here's what I'm wearing'?

### Script beats
- Show the full outfit with a confident pose or spin.
- Zoom in on key details: texture, accessories, or unique elements.
- Demonstrate one practical aspect, like pocket functionality or movement.
- End with how the outfit makes you feel or where you'd wear it.

### Caption / title
OOTD captions that tell a story beyond the clothes.

### CTA
Which caption fits your style vibe today? Save for later!

### Hashtags
#OOTD #FashionTikTok #StyleInspo #OutfitIdeas #FashionCreator #WhatImWearing #DailyOutfit #StyleTips #WardrobeEssentials #FashionHacks #ThriftedFinds #CapsuleWardrobe #AffordableFashion #PersonalStyle

### Why it works
- Captions create context, making the outfit relatable and shareable.
- Story-driven hooks increase watch time and engagement rates.
- Niche-aware language attracts a targeted, style-interested audience.
