## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your pet videos? I've got 50 that actually work.

### Script beats
- Quick intro on the universal struggle of writing captions.
- Show a rapid-fire montage of 3-5 cute pet clips with different captions from the list on screen.
- Explain how a good caption prompts comments and shares.
- Direct viewers to save the video for their own caption ideas.
- End with a funny pet blooper related to a caption.

### Caption / title
50 TikTok Captions for Pet Accounts (Save this for later!)

### CTA
Which number caption is your favorite? Save this for your next video!

- Solves a real, common problem for pet creators (caption writer's block).
