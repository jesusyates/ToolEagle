## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using generic POV captions. Here are 50 that make people watch.

### Script beats
- Start with a strong, specific visual scenario in the first three seconds.
- Show your genuine reaction to the situation—confusion, joy, surprise.
- Keep the camera close to capture subtle facial expressions.
- Use quick cuts or a zoom to emphasize the pivotal moment.
- End on a satisfying resolution or an open-ended question.

### Caption / title
POV captions that get 80% video completion rates.

### Why it works
- POV frames drop viewers directly into an emotional or situational context.
- It triggers curiosity about the outcome, boosting average watch time.
- Relatable scenarios prompt shares and comments with personal stories.
