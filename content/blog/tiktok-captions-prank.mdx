## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to caption your prank videos so they actually stop the scroll?

### Script beats
- Open with the target's normal activity to establish context.
- Show the prank setup quickly without giving away the punchline.
- Capture the genuine reaction in a tight, clear shot.
- End with your own laughter or a reveal of the mechanism.
- Use a quick cut to text explaining the harmless nature.
