## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to describe your product in a way that sells?

### Script beats
- Show the product solving a specific, relatable pain point.
- Highlight one unique feature or benefit clearly.
- Demonstrate the result or transformation quickly.
- Address a common doubt or question head-on.
- End with a clear, low-pressure next step.

### Caption / title
The right caption frames your product as the obvious solution.

### CTA
Which product should I review next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#ProductTok #AmazonFinds #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #Unboxing #LifeHack #ProductReview #Gadget #HomeImprovement #CleanTok #BeautyTok #TechTok #KitchenHacks #MustHave #AsSeenOnTikTok

### Why it works
- Focuses on a tangible outcome or solved problem.
- Uses simple, conversational language viewers trust.
- Encourages interaction with a question or prompt.
