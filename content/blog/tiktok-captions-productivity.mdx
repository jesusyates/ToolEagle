## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your captions are losing you views. Here's the fix.

### Script beats
- Show a common productivity mistake in the first 3 seconds.
- Quick cut to you implementing the simple fix or alternative.
- On-screen text highlights the key benefit or time saved.
- End with a direct question to the viewer about their habit.

### Caption / title
You're managing your time wrong. Try this instead.

### CTA
Which tip are you trying first? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#productivity #productivitytips #timemanagement #workfromhome #studytok #routine #morningroutine #efficiency #procrastination #focus #selfimprovement #goals #planning #organization

### Why it works
- Addresses a universal pain point (lack of time/ focus).
- Offers a simple, actionable swap, not just theory.
- Encourages conversation with a direct call to comment.
