## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get comments? The right question in your caption can turn passive viewers into active participants.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable frustration about low engagement.
- Introduce the power of a strategic question to break the scroll.
- Quickly showcase 2-3 compelling examples from the list.
- Explain how to adapt a question to their specific content.
- Direct them to the full list and caption tool for more.

### Caption / title
50 Questions to Ask in Your TikTok Captions

### CTA
Pick a question, adapt it to your video, and test it today.

### Hashtags
#TikTokCaptions #ContentCreatorTips #SocialMediaStrategy #EngagementHacks #QuestionCaptions #TikTokGrowth #CreatorToolkit #VideoMarketing #ContentIdeas #AudienceEngagement #TikTokTips #GoViral #DigitalCreator #ContentCreation

### Why it works
- Questions prompt a direct response, lowering the barrier to comment.
- They make the viewer an active participant in the content.
- Algorithm-friendly, as comments signal high engagement.
- Builds community by starting conversations around shared experiences.
