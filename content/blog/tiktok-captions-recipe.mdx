## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your recipe videos?

### Script beats
- Show the finished dish looking amazing
- Quick cuts of the simple steps
- Close-up of the best bite
- Text overlay with the key benefit or time save

### Caption / title
You only need 5 ingredients for this creamy pasta.

### CTA
Save this for your next pasta night and tell me if you try it!

### Hashtags
#RecipeTok #FoodTok #EasyRecipes #CookingHacks #QuickMeals #HomeCook #Foodie #DinnerIdeas #MealPrep #CookingTips #BudgetMeals #ASMRcooking

### Why it works
- Focuses on a clear benefit like speed or simplicity
- Uses curiosity gaps with 'secret' or 'hack'
- Directly addresses common viewer pain points
