## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that actually get comments? Here are 50 proven formulas.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, silent clip of a universal struggle (e.g., spilling coffee).
- On-screen text: 'When this happens...'
- Cut to your reaction face.
- Text: 'Caption idea below ↓'
- End with a direct question to the viewer related to the clip.

### Caption / title
Save this for when you can't think of a caption. Which one do you relate to most?

### CTA
Comment which number you're saving and why!

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Taps into universal human experiences, lowering the barrier to comment.
- The 'save this' framing provides immediate utility, encouraging saves.
- Asking viewers to pick a number is a low-effort, high-response CTA.
- The list format promises clear value, reducing scroll-away rate.
