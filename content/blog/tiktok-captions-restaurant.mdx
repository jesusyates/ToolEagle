## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that make people save your restaurant videos?

### Script beats
- Start with a close-up of your most visually stunning dish.
- Quick cut to the chef or a unique preparation step.
- End with the finished plated dish and a text overlay with the key detail.
- On-screen text should highlight the specific, niche detail from the caption.

### Caption / title
The detail in the caption is what makes people hit 'save'.

### CTA
Which detail would make your audience save this? Try our caption generator for more.

### Hashtags
#RestaurantTok #FoodieFinds #HiddenGems #FoodContent #TikTokFood #LocalEats #FoodCaptions #RestaurantMarketing #FoodTikTok #SaveThisRecipe #DishDetails #FoodCreator

### Why it works
- Specific, sensory details create desire and credibility.
- Niche techniques or ingredients appeal to a dedicated food audience.
- 'Save-worthy' content often answers 'how' or 'why' something is special.
