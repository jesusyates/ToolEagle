## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your video is great, but is your caption holding it back? Here's how to fix it.

### Script beats
- Identify the core emotion or value of your clip.
- Choose a caption structure that amplifies that feeling.
- Inject a specific detail or question to prompt interaction.
- End with a clear, low-friction call to action.
- Double-check that the tone matches your on-screen energy.
