## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to turn views into sales? Your caption might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Show the product solving a clear pain point in the first 3 seconds.
- Display social proof like reviews or sold-out notifications.
- Visually highlight the discount or limited-time offer.
- End with a clear, low-friction call to action.
- Use text overlays to reinforce the key selling point.

### Caption / title
The caption is your silent salesperson. Make it work harder.

### CTA
Tap the link in bio before this deal disappears.

### Hashtags
#TikTokShop #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #SmallBusinessCheck #SaleAlert #DiscountCode #LimitedStock #MustHave #ShoppingTikTok #Dropshipping #EcomTips #FlashSale #BuyNow

### Why it works
- Creates urgency with time-sensitive or quantity-limited language.
- Lowers perceived risk by incorporating social proof and guarantees.
- Directs intent with a clear, simple next step for the viewer.
