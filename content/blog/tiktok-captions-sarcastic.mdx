### Hook
Struggling to caption your sarcastic TikTok? Stop overthinking. Here's a proven formula to package your wit for maximum engagement, complete with a ready-to-shoot script outline.

### Script beats
- Open with a deadpan look to camera or an overly-dramatic reenactment of a relatable struggle.
- Cut to text overlay or quick visual gag that highlights the ironic twist or punchline.
