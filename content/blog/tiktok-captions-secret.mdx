## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
What if you knew a secret that could change everything?

### Script beats
- Tease the secret without giving it away immediately.
- Show the process of discovery or the hidden object.
- Reveal the secret with a satisfying visual or explanation.
- Ask viewers if they have a similar secret to share.

### Caption / title
I can't believe I'm sharing this secret...

### CTA
What's a secret you've never told anyone? Comment below.

### Hashtags
#secret #secrettips #hiddenknowledge #lifehacks #didyouknow #mystery #tipsandtricks #secretrecipe #hiddenhistory #unsolvedmysteries #creatortips #tiktoktips #viralhacks #learnontiktok

### Why it works
- Secrets trigger innate human curiosity and the fear of missing out.
- The format promises a valuable payoff, encouraging viewers to watch fully.
- It invites personal connection by asking viewers to share their own secrets.
