## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I asked a derm for the most common skincare mistake. You're probably making it.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: 'The #1 Skincare Mistake According to a Derm'
- Quick cut to creator looking shocked or guilty.
- Demonstrate the incorrect method (e.g., over-exfoliating, wrong product order).
- Immediately show the correct, gentle method with clear labels.
- Side-by-side comparison of skin results (or use graphics).

### Caption / title
Stop damaging your skin barrier. Do this instead.

### CTA
Which mistake were you making? Tell me below.

### Hashtags
#skincare #skincaretips #dermtips #skincareroutine #beautytips #skincaremistake #skinbarrier #glowingskin #skincare101 #beautyhack #tiktokbeauty #skincaretok

### Why it works
- Uses authority ('derm') to build immediate credibility and curiosity.
- Identifies a common pain point, making it highly relatable.
- Provides a clear, actionable solution within the short video format.
- The CTA is low-effort and encourages comments, boosting engagement.
