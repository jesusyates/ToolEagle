## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I run a small business. Here are 50 captions I actually use.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: '50 TikTok Captions for Small Biz'
- Quick cuts showing 3-5 different caption examples on screen
- Point to camera: 'Save this for your next video.'
- End screen with a call to action and your handle

### Caption / title
50 TikTok Captions for Small Business Owners (Save this list!)

### CTA
Which caption will you try first? Save this for your next video idea.

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Provides immediate, tangible value with a high number of usable ideas.
- Targets a specific, active audience (small business owners on TikTok).
- Uses a listicle format that is easy to consume and encourages saving.
