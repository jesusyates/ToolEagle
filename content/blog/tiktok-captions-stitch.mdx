## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your Stitch is ready, but a weak caption kills its potential. Here's how to fix it.

### Script beats
- Open with a hook that poses a question or presents a conflict.
- Deliver your core point or reaction clearly in the first 3 seconds.
- End your video with a clear, actionable invitation for a Stitch.
- Use on-screen text to reinforce the Stitch prompt.

### Caption / title
Stitch this if you have a better answer.

### CTA
Stitch with your take and tag me!

### Hashtags
#TikTokStitch #StitchChallenge #ContentCreator #TikTokTips #ViralTikTok #EngagementHacks #SocialMediaStrategy #CreatorEconomy #TikTokGrowth #VideoMarketing #ContentStrategy #DigitalCreator

### Why it works
- Directly asks for a specific type of user-generated content (UGC).
- Frames the Stitch as a collaboration, not just a reply.
- Uses conflict or curiosity to trigger the impulse to respond.
