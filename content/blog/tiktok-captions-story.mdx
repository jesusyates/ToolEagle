## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to start your story? These captions are your instant hook.

### Script beats
- Paste a caption that fits your video.
- Record your story matching the prompt's energy.
- Use the caption verbatim or tweak it slightly.
- Add relevant hashtags from the list below.
- Post and track which stories get the most saves.

### Caption / title
My most embarrassing moment involved a karaoke machine and my boss.

### CTA
Which story will you tell first? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#Storytime #TikTokStory #PersonalStory #TrueStory #Storytelling #LifeLesson #RealTalk #ViralStory #StoryCaption #Narrative #EngagingContent #CreatorTips #StoryPrompt #ContentIdea

### Why it works
- Prompts are specific, reducing creator's blank-page anxiety.
- Taps into proven relatable human experiences for connection.
- Directly aligns with TikTok's preference for authentic, personal content.
