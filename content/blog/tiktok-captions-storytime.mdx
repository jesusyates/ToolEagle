## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop viewers mid-scroll with a caption that promises a personal, intriguing, or unbelievable story.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable but curious situation.
- Build tension with unexpected twists or details.
- Reveal the core conflict or humorous dilemma.
- End on a cliffhanger or a reflective question.

### Caption / title
You won't believe what happened next...

### CTA
What's the wildest story that's ever happened to you? Share below!

### Hashtags
#storytime #tiktokstory #viralstories #personalanecdote #funnystory #crazystory #storytelling #fyp #foryou #relatable #unbelievable #truecrime #comedy #drama

### Why it works
- Human curiosity drives viewers to seek resolution.
- Personal stories foster community and relatability.
- The 'what happens next' hook maximizes watch time.
