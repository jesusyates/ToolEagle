## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your study content? I've got 50 ready for you to copy.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: '50 Study Captions You Can Steal.'
- Quick cuts showing 3-5 different caption examples on screen.
- Creator talking to camera: 'These are designed for saves and shares.'
- Show the 'POV: You just found the perfect caption' trend.
- End screen with CTA to save the video for later.

### Caption / title
50 captions for your next study video. Save this for later!

### CTA
Which number is your favorite? Drop it below & save for your next study session!

### Hashtags
#studytok #studygram #studytips #studentlife #captionideas #contentcreator #studyinspo #productivity #academic #edutok #studyhacks #learnontiktok #studymotivation

### Why it works
- Solves a specific, common pain point (caption writer's block) for a niche community.
- The high number (50) promises immense value, encouraging saves and shares.
- Directly uses TikTok's 'save for later' culture in the hook and CTA.
- The captions are concrete, actionable, and framed for viewer benefit.
