## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
(Hold up a common gadget, like a phone charger) This is why your stuff keeps breaking. And it's not the cable's fault.

### Script beats
- Quick close-up of a frayed or generic charging cable.
- Cut to a simple graphic or text overlay: 'Voltage Irregularity'.
- Show a proper, certified charger (like from the device maker).
- Demonstrate using a multi-meter app or just contrast device charging speeds.
- End with a shot of a healthy battery percentage graphic.

### Caption / title
Your cheap charger is slowly killing your battery.

### CTA
What's the oldest gadget you're still using?

### Hashtags
#TechTip #LifeHack #BatteryHealth #TechTok #GadgetHack #SaveMoney #DIYTech #PhoneBattery #TechExplained #LearnOnTikTok #DidYouKnow #TechAdvice

### Why it works
- Identifies a common, frustrating pain point (broken gear, poor battery).
- Uses a simple, visual before/after or problem/solution structure.
- The CTA is low-effort and encourages personal story sharing in comments.
- Hashtags blend broad reach (#TechTok) with specific search (#BatteryHealth).
