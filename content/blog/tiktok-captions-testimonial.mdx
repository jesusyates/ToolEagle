## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop writing boring testimonial captions that get scrolled past.

### Script beats
- Show the specific problem you had before
- Describe the moment you realized the solution worked
- Share the measurable result or emotional benefit
- Address common skepticism your audience might feel
- End with who else would benefit from this

### Caption / title
When a product actually lives up to the hype. #TestimonialTea

### CTA
Which of these results would help your audience most?

### Hashtags
#Testimonial #ProductReview #BeforeAndAfter #HonestReview #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #CustomerTestimonial #TryItYourself #ResultsDontLie #GameChanger #WorthTheMoney #SatisfiedCustomer #LifeChanging #MustHave #CreatorTools

### Why it works
- Social proof reduces perceived risk for viewers
- Specific results feel more authentic than vague praise
- Story format keeps viewers engaged until the end
