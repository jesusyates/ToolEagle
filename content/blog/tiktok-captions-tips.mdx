## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop leaving views and comments on the table. Your caption is half the battle.

### Script beats
- On-screen text: 'Your Caption is a Call to Action.'
- Quick cuts showing 3 different videos with boring vs. engaging captions.
- Hold up a phone showing a comment section flooded with replies.
- Say: 'I tested 50 formulas. These are the ones that actually work.'

### Caption / title
50 TikTok Caption Formulas That Actually Get Comments & Shares

### CTA
Save this list and try 3 new formulas this week.

### Hashtags
#tiktokcaptions #contentcreator #socialmediatips #digitalmarketing #contentstrategy #tiktokgrowth #engagementtips #creatorlife #socialmediamarketing #contentcreation #tiktoktips #videomarketing #captionideas #contentideas

### Why it works
- Solves a specific, common pain point (low engagement) with a tangible solution (formulas).
- The high number (50) promises immense value, encouraging saves and shares.
- The hook directly addresses the creator's desire for better performance, stopping the scroll.
