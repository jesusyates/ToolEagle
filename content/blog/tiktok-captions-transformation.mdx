## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to caption your transformation videos? These 50 captions are proven to work.

### Script beats
- Show the dramatic before shot for immediate intrigue.
- Use quick cuts to highlight key steps in the process.
- End with a satisfying reveal of the final transformed result.
- Include a close-up on the most improved detail.
- Add text overlays to emphasize the time or effort invested.

### Caption / title
The transformation you didn't see coming starts right here.

### CTA
Which transformation will you try next? Save this for ideas.

### Hashtags
#Transformation #Makeover #BeforeAndAfter #DIYProjects #SelfImprovement #LifeHack #ProcessVideo #Satisfying #GrowthMindset #CreativeProcess #HowTo #TikTokTips #ContentCreator

### Why it works
- Taps into the universally compelling 'before and after' narrative.
- Encourages saves and shares by offering tangible value or inspiration.
- Aligns with TikTok's preference for satisfying, progress-driven content.
