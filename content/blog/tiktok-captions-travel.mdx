## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to caption your travel TikToks? Your caption is the handshake that invites viewers into your journey. A great one can turn a scroll into a connection.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick, visually striking shot of your location.
- Cut to a close-up of your reaction or a unique detail.
- Show a brief, relatable struggle or a moment of awe.
- End on the wider view or a human moment that sums up the vibe.
