## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions for your tutorial Reels?

### Script beats
- Show the problem visually in the first 3 seconds.
- Demonstrate your quick fix or method clearly.
- Highlight the key benefit or time saved.
- End with a clear call to action.

### Caption / title
How to [Achieve Benefit] in [Short Timeframe]

### CTA
Save this for your next project and try it!

### Hashtags
#TutorialReel #HowToTikTok #LifeHacks #DIYTutorial LearnOnTikTok #EasyTutorial #TipsAndTricks #CreatorTips #TikTokTips #ContentCreation #SkillShare #DoItYourself #QuickTips #HelpfulHacks

### Why it works
- Captions frame the tutorial's value proposition clearly.
- Action-oriented language prompts viewers to engage and save.
- Hashtags help surface content to learners searching for help.
