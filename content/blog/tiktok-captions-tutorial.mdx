## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your tutorial is great, but a weak caption is killing your views. Here are 50 formulas to fix that.

### Script beats
- Show a quick before/after of a boring vs. engaging caption on screen.
- Explain that the caption is the call to action for your video's value.
- Rapid-fire scroll through 5-7 of the most versatile caption formulas.
- Pick one formula and break down exactly why it triggers engagement.
- Tell viewers to save the list and use one on their next video.

### Caption / title
50 Caption Formulas For Tutorials That Get Real Engagement

### CTA
Which number will you try first? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#tiktoktutorial #captiontips #contentcreator #socialmediatips #digitalmarketing #tiktokgrowth #contentstrategy #engagementtips #howtotiktok #creatorlife #videomarketing #tiktokcaption #contentcreation #tiktoktips

### Why it works
- Solves a specific, common pain point (low engagement) with a tangible solution (50 formulas).
- The high number (50) implies immense value and saves the viewer time searching.
- The hook creates a problem ('killing your views') and immediately offers the fix.
- The CTA is low-effort and encourages immediate interaction in the comments.
