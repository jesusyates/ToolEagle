## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I spent $100 on a viral product so you don't have to.

### Script beats
- Show the branded box with high anticipation
- Focus on the sounds and textures of opening
- Reveal the product with genuine first reaction
- Quickly highlight key features and initial impressions
- End with a question to prompt comments

### Caption / title
Testing a viral product: does it live up to the hype?

### CTA
Would you buy this? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#unboxing #productreview #tiktokmademebuyit #satisfying #asmr #packaging #shoppinghaul #viralproduct #firstimpressions #newfavorite #deliveryday #onlineshopping #trending

### Why it works
- Leverages curiosity about viral products
- Uses satisfying sensory triggers (ASMR)
- Encourages community interaction with a direct CTA
- Taps into the relatable experience of online shopping
