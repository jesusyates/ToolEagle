## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using generic captions for your vacation TikToks. Here are 50 hooks that actually work.

### Script beats
- Show a quick, stunning vacation clip.
- Text overlay asks a question about travel feelings.
- Cut to you looking relaxed or amazed.
- Use a trending sound that fits wanderlust.
- End with a call to engage in the comments.

### Caption / title
Which of these vacation vibes are you?

### CTA
Save this for your next trip!

### Hashtags
#TravelTikTok #VacationMode #Wanderlust #TravelCaptions #BucketList #TravelInspo #VacationVibes #PassportReady #ExploreMore #TravelContent #DigitalNomad #TravelCreator #AdventureAwaits #SeeTheWorld

### Why it works
- Captions evoke specific, relatable emotions instead of generic statements.
- They encourage viewers to project their own travel dreams onto the content.
- Mix of aspirational and humorous tones caters to different audience segments.
- Directly prompts saves and shares by being a useful, evergreen resource.
