## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using boring captions on your comparison videos.

### Script beats
- Show the two items or concepts you're comparing clearly.
- State your own preference or result briefly.
- Ask viewers directly which side they are on.
- Encourage them to debate their choice in the comments.
- Use text overlays to emphasize key differences.

### Caption / title
You have to pick one side. Which is it?

### CTA
Comment your choice and tag a friend who needs to see this.

### Hashtags
#vs #debate #comparison #tiktoktips #contentcreator #engagement #viral #fyp #trending #pickone #discussion #opinion #contentstrategy

### Why it works
- Forces a binary choice, making it easy for viewers to engage.
- Taps into personal identity and preference, driving emotional responses.
- The debate format naturally boosts comments and shareability.
