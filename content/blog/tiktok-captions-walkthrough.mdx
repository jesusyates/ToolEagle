## Walkthrough Captions

1. Use a rhetorical question to spark immediate viewer curiosity.
2. Pair a surprising fact with a direct call to action.
3. Start with 'POV:' to instantly create a relatable scenario.
4. Contrast a common mistake with your pro tip solution.
5. Tease a valuable secret you're about to reveal in the video.
6. Frame your caption as a urgent public service announcement.
7. Ask for a specific emoji reaction to gauge audience sentiment.
8. Use 'Wait for it...' to build anticipation for the video payoff.
9. Challenge a widely held belief within your specific niche.
10. Share a micro-story in three sentences max to set the scene.
11. State a controversial opinion to fuel discussion in comments.
12. Create a two-part caption with setup and punchline structure.
13. Directly address your ideal viewer with 'For the people who...'.
14. Use all caps for one key emotional word to emphasize feeling.
15. Incorporate a trending audio prompt directly into your text.
16. Pose a 'this or that' choice relevant to your content theme.
17. Hint at a personal struggle you overcame in the video topic.
18. Start with 'Signs you...' to trigger self-identification from viewers.
19. Use brackets to add context like [sound on] or [wait for it].
20. Quote the most compelling line from your video's audio.
21. Make a bold prediction about a trend in your industry.
22. Share a tiny hack that saves time for your niche audience.
23. Confess a common creator fear to build authenticity and trust.
24. Use number sequencing like 'Step 1:' to imply a quick tutorial.
25. Ask a 'yes or no' question that's easy to answer in comments.
26. Reveal the one tool you can't live without for your craft.
27. Compare before and after states in a single, compelling line.
28. Give permission to do something your audience feels guilty about.
29. Use slang or jargon that only your niche community understands.
30. End with an open loop prompting viewers to ask for part two.
