## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
POV: You've just been asked to turn your camera on during a WFH meeting.

### Script beats
- Quick cut to a perfectly framed upper body in a blazer.
- Pan down to reveal pajama pants and fluffy slippers.
- Show a frantic scramble to find a 'professional' background.
- End with a perfectly timed, slightly strained smile to the camera.

### Caption / title
The WFH reality no one talks about. #workfromhometruths

### CTA
Which WFH struggle is the most real? Drop a comment below!

### Hashtags
#workfromhome #wfh #remotework #homeoffice #officelife #productivity #dayinmylife #corporatetok #9to5 #desksetup #remotejobs #worklife #dailyroutine #officetok

### Why it works
- Relatable to millions of remote and hybrid workers.
- Uses visual comedy (the pajama pant reveal) which performs well on TikTok.
- Encourages engagement by asking viewers to share their own experience.
- Taps into the shared, unspoken rituals of modern work culture.
