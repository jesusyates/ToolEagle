## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling with what to post? You don't need a complex setup. Here's a complete, ready-to-use script for a high-potential beginner video.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable problem or question (e.g., 'Does anyone else's brain do this?').
- Visually demonstrate the scenario with quick cuts or a simple POV shot.
- Offer your simple tip, hack, or reaction to the situation.
- End with a genuine, curious expression or a nod to the camera.

### Caption / title
Trying this beginner-friendly format. What's your go-to simple video idea?

### CTA
