## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get your controversial takes seen? The right hashtags can place your video in the center of the debate.

### Script beats
- Open with a bold, clear statement on your chosen topic.
- Present the two opposing sides of the argument fairly but briefly.
- State your personal stance or perspective with supporting reasoning.
- Pose a direct question to the audience to spark comments.
- End with a call to action for viewers to share their own views.

### Caption / title
The debate no one can agree on... what do YOU think?

### CTA
Drop your take in the comments—let's discuss respectfully.

### Hashtags
#ControversialTopics #DebateTok #HotTake #Opinion #PoliticsTok #SocialCommentary #DeepConversations #WhatDoYouThink #LetDebate #UnpopularOpinion #ThoughtProvoking #CurrentEvents #SpeakYourMind

### Why it works
- Taps into high-engagement 'debate' and 'opinion' communities on TikTok.
- Algorithm favors content that sparks comments and extended watch time.
- Clear framing invites audience participation, boosting video metrics.
