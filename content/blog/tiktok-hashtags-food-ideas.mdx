## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using generic food hashtags that get lost in millions of posts.

### Script beats
- Show a quick clip of your finished dish
- Flash text overlay of 3-5 key hashtags you'll use
- Explain why these specific hashtags work for this niche
- Show analytics or engagement from using targeted hashtags
- End with CTA to save for their next food video

### Caption / title
These food hashtag strategies helped my recipes reach 10x more foodies

### CTA
Save this list for your next cooking video and test 3 new combinations

### Hashtags
#foodtok #recipeideas #cookingtips #foodcontent #kitchentok #easyrecipes #foodhacks #mealprep #cookingtutorial #foodvideo #recipevideo #cookingtok #foodcreator

- Targets users actively searching for specific recipe types
- Combines broad reach hashtags with niche discovery tags
