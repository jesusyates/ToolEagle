## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your hashtags are probably hurting your TikTok reach. Here's how to fix them.

### Script beats
- Show a video with terrible, generic hashtags like #fyp #viral.
- Cut to a video using a strategic mix from the list, showing higher views.
- On-screen text lists the three key hashtag types every caption needs.
- Quick cuts of 3-4 different niche examples from the full list.
- End with a call to action to save the list for your next video.
