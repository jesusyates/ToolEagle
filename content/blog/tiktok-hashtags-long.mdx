## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get views on your longer TikTok videos? The right hashtag strategy can dramatically increase your reach.

### Script beats
- Identify your video's core topic and target audience.
- Select 3-5 primary hashtags from relevant categories below.
- Mix in 2-3 trending but relevant hashtags for discovery.
- Include 1-2 niche hashtags specific to your content style.
- Analyze performance and adjust your strategy weekly.

### Caption / title
Master Long-Form TikTok: The Ultimate Hashtag Strategy Guide

Copy your favorite sets below and test them on your next long video.

- Targets users specifically searching for detailed, educational content.
- Balances broad reach hashtags with specific niche identifiers.
