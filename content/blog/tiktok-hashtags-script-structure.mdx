## Script Structure Hashtags

2. Include two to three secondary hashtags describing the content format.
3. Add one or two trending hashtags relevant to your niche this week.
4. Incorporate a branded hashtag unique to your creator channel.
5. Place the most specific hashtags first in your caption list.
6. Limit your total hashtags to a focused set of five to eight.

## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop just adding hashtags. Start scripting FOR them. Here's the structure.

### Script beats
- Identify one primary discovery hashtag for your core topic.
- Select supporting hashtags for format, audience, and trend.
- Write your script's hook to directly serve the primary hashtag's intent.
- Deliver content that matches the expectations of those searching the hashtag.
- End with a clear CTA that leverages the community you just targeted.

### Caption / title
The Hashtag-First Script Structure for TikTok

### CTA
Use our free tool to generate a hashtag set for your next video.

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Forces creator intent by aligning content with searchable topics.
- Signals clear context to the algorithm about audience and relevance.
- Builds a predictable framework for scalable content production.
