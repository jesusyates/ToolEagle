### Hook
Struggling to find the right hashtags that actually get your Reels seen?

### Script beats
- Identify your video's core niche and primary audience
- Select 3-5 primary hashtags from relevant high-volume categories
- Add 5-8 secondary hashtags targeting specific communities or trends
- Include 2-4 branded or campaign-specific hashtags for consistency
- Test different combinations and track performance weekly
