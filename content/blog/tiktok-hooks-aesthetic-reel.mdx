## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your aesthetic content is beautiful, but is it stopping the scroll?

### Script beats
- Quickly state the common visual problem your hook addresses.
- Show a 2-second 'before' glimpse (slightly off composition/lighting).
- Reveal the simple fix with a satisfying visual result.
- End on a final, polished shot that embodies the tip.

### Caption / title
A simple fix for a more polished feed.

### CTA
Which visual tip should I break down next?

### Hashtags
#aesthetictiktok #contentcreatortips #visualstorytelling #tiktokhooks #contentstrategy #videotips #creatortools #digitalcreator #contentideas #videography #socialmediatips #creativeprocess

### Why it works
- Addresses a specific, felt pain point for visual creators.
- Promises a tangible improvement that is quick to implement.
- Leverages the satisfying 'before and after' format TikTok loves.
