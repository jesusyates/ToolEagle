## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your branded hashtag is failing because your hook is an afterthought.

### Script beats
- Show the common, boring way brands introduce their hashtag.
- Flip to reveal the psychological reason it doesn't work.
- Demonstrate the 'insider' hook that makes people want to participate.
- Show the tangible result (more UGC, higher reach).
- End with a clear, simple action using the branded hashtag.

### Caption / title
Your Brand Hashtag Hook Is Probably All Wrong

### CTA
Try this hook formula with your own brand hashtag in the comments.

### Hashtags
#BrandStrategy #TikTokMarketing #SocialMediaTips #ContentCreator #DigitalMarketing #BrandBuilding #HashtagStrategy #ViralHooks #TikTokGrowth #MarketingTips #SmallBusinessTips #ContentStrategy

### Why it works
- Shifts focus from brand promotion to viewer benefit.
- Uses pattern interruption to break through scroll fatigue.
- Frames participation as gaining status or solving a problem.
