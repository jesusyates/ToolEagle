## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get your business content seen on TikTok?

### Script beats
- State a common business problem your audience faces.
- Reveal a counterintuitive insight or a personal failure story.
- Show the positive outcome after applying your suggested method.
- Pose a provocative question that challenges standard advice.
- End with a clear, actionable tip or a teaser for more.

### Caption / title

### CTA
