## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your business content is invisible without a hook that speaks directly to your ideal client's pain point.

### Script beats
- Open with a bold, specific claim about a common business mistake or result.
- Quickly establish your credibility with a niche-specific result or client example.
- Tease the valuable insight or framework you'll share, making it feel exclusive.
- Use on-screen text or a quick visual to emphasize the key data point or step.
- End with an implied promise of transformation if they watch the full video.

### Caption / title
These hooks are engineered for business creators who sell services, courses, or products. Each one targets a specific financial, operational, or marketing pain point that B2B or B2C decision-makers experience, moving beyond generic advice into actionable, niche-aware statements.

### Why it works
- Uses concrete numbers, timelines, and outcomes to build immediate credibility and curiosity.
- Targets high-stakes business problems (money, time, clients) to ensure the right audience stops scrolling.
- Frames the advice as a 'mistake to avoid' or 'secret weapon,' triggering a fear-of-missing-out response.
- The specificity (e.g., 'for SaaS founders,' 'local bakery') helps the algorithm surface it to a defined niche.
