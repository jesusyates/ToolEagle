## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
My brain trying to remember a simple password.

### Script beats
- Start with a confused, blank stare at the camera.
- Mime frantic typing followed by head-in-hands frustration.
- Cut to a flashback of creating an overly complex password.
- Return to present with a shrug and acceptance of failure.

### Caption / title
The password struggle is too real. #RelatableComedy

### CTA
Which password rule do you always forget?

### Hashtags
#ComedyReel #TikTokComedy #Relatable #FunnyVideos #SketchComedy #EverydayStruggles #Humor #ContentCreator #ViralHooks #ShortForm #DigitalComedy #StandUp #Improve #FunnyContent

### Why it works
- Taps into a near-universal moment of frustration.
- Uses physical comedy which performs well without sound.
- Leaves room for audience participation in the comments.
