## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I asked the internet to name this stray kitten and the winning name was perfect.

### Script beats
- Show a cute, curious stray kitten looking at the camera.
- On-screen text: 'You guys named her!'
- Reveal the chosen name with a cute graphic.
- Show the now-named kitten being playful or cozy.
- End with the kitten snuggling or a final cute pose.

### Caption / title
You named her, and she loves her new name!

### CTA
What should I name next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#cute #kitten #catsoftiktok #petnames #aesthetic #cozy #wholesome #fyp #foryou #viral #trending #pets #animalsoftiktok #dailydoseofcute

### Why it works
- Uses audience participation, which boosts comments and engagement.
- Centers on an universally appealing subject (a cute animal).
- Creates a narrative payoff by revealing the community-chosen name.
- Ends on a satisfying and emotionally positive visual.
