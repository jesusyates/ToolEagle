## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling. I bet you can't guess which dance move is secretly the hardest.

### Script beats
- Quick close-up on a confused face
- Demonstrate a seemingly simple move
- Reveal the tricky technical element with a slow-mo replay
- Challenge viewer to try it themselves
- End with a smooth transition into your full dance

### Caption / title
The dance move that looks easy but will humble you.

### CTA
Try it and tag me so I can see your attempt!

### Hashtags
#DanceHooks #TikTokDance #DanceChallenge #Choreography #DanceTips #ViralDance #DanceContent #FYP #DanceRoutine #LearnToDance #DanceMoves #DanceTok

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gap by hinting at a secret
- Creates a direct challenge to the viewer
- Showcases skill while being relatable and instructional
