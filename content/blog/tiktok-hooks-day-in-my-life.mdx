## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I let an AI plan my entire workday and the results were surprising.

### Script beats
- Quick cut of me reading the AI-generated schedule with a skeptical look
- Montage of attempting the first few bizarre AI-suggested tasks
- Midday check-in showing clear confusion and a messy workspace
- Final reveal of one unexpectedly brilliant productivity tip I discovered
- End screen asking viewers what their perfect workday looks like

### Caption / title
When AI Plans Your Day: A Creator Experiment

### CTA
What would an AI schedule for YOUR perfect day? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#DayInMyLife #ContentCreator #ProductivityHacks #WorkFromHome #TikTokCreator #Routine #AITools #CreatorLife #MorningRoutine #ProductivityTips #ContentCreation #DigitalCreator #Workday #Vlog

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gap about AI and daily life
- Relatable premise for any creator with a chaotic schedule
- Allows for humorous and authentic reaction shots
- Ends with a clear, engaging question for comments

## Best for TikTok

1. Lifestyle and productivity creators
2. Vloggers and routine-based content
3. Creators seeking higher engagement
