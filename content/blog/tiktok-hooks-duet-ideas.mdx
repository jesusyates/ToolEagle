### Hook
Struggling to get duets? Here are 30 hooks that actually work.

### Script beats
- Pick a hook that fits your niche and style.
- Set up your shot with clear visual cues.
- Perform your part with authentic energy.
- End with a clear invitation to duet.
- Post and engage with everyone who duets.
