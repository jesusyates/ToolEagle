## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I spent 100 hours to learn this one broken game mechanic.

### Script beats
- Quick cut to failing repeatedly at the task.
- Show the 'aha' moment of discovering the trick.
- Demonstrate the mechanic working flawlessly.
- Reaction shot of relief or shock at the result.
- Text overlay asking if viewers want a full tutorial.

### Caption / title
The game doesn't want you to know this shortcut.

### CTA
What game should I break next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#LetsPlay #GamingTips #GameGlitch #GamingTikTok #Speedrun #GamingCommunity #IndieGames #GameMechanics #BossFight #GamingSetup #PCGaming #GamingClips #GameDev #GamerLife

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gap about a hidden advantage.
- Showcases dedication and provides immediate value.
- Fast-paced visual proof keeps viewers watching.
- Encourages interaction through questions and comments.
