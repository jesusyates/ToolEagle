## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
You think you know your town, but you're wrong. Let me show you the secret it's hiding.

### Script beats
- Quick shot of a seemingly normal suburban street.
- Close-up on a strange object or symbol hidden in plain sight.
- Reveal a disturbing piece of local history with a text overlay.
- End with a direct, questioning look at the camera.

### Caption / title
The secret my town doesn't want you to know. #Mystery

### CTA
What's the weirdest thing about where YOU live? Comment below.

### Hashtags
#Mystery #Unsolved #Creepy #UrbanLegend #HiddenHistory #TrueCrime #Paranormal #Thriller #Storytime #Conspiracy #Dark #Secrets #Unexplained #Curiosity

### Why it works
- Taps into universal curiosity about the unknown and hidden truths.
- Uses local, relatable settings to make the mystery feel personal and immediate.
- Leaves a clear gap of knowledge that demands an answer or theory from the viewer.
