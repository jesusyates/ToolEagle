## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your content is for everyone, so why is no one watching?

### Script beats
- State a common frustration in your niche.
- Introduce a highly specific solution or 'secret'.
- Hint at the negative consequence of not knowing this.
- Visually demonstrate the key tip or result.
- End with a question prompting a niche-specific answer.

### Caption / title
The niche mistake you don't know you're making.

### CTA
What's your biggest niche struggle? Comment below.

### Hashtags
#NicheTok #MicroCommunity #SpecificNotGeneric #DeepDive #ExpertTips #HobbyistLife #PassionProject #DIYCommunity #LearnWithMe #EduTok #HowToTok #ProTips #TikTokTips #ContentStrategy

### Why it works
- Filters out casual scrollers, attracting viewers genuinely interested in the topic.
- Uses hashtags as direct signals to TikTok's algorithm about your core audience.
- Positions the creator as a knowledgeable insider within a specific community.
- Sparks detailed comments and discussions from a highly engaged micro-audience.

1. Creators targeting micro-communities
2. Accounts struggling with broad reach
