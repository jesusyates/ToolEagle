## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
POV: You're the quiet one who knows everyone's secrets.

### Script beats
- Establish the 'you' character with a quick visual cue.
- Show the internal conflict through a reactive glance or expression.
- Reveal the secret knowledge with a subtle, knowing look to camera.

### Caption / title
The quiet observer sees all. #POV #StoryTime

### CTA
Which secret would be hardest to keep?

### Hashtags
#POV #StoryTime #Relatable #FYP #Viral #Comedy #Acting #Skit #Scenario #PlotTwist #SkitTok #StoryTok #Character

### Why it works
- POV instantly places the viewer in the story, increasing engagement.
- Relatable scenarios trigger strong 'that's me' reactions from the audience.
- The mystery of 'the secret' creates immediate curiosity for the follow-up.
