## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your caption is the first thing people read. Make it impossible to ignore.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable pain point or curiosity gap.
- Present your key insight or method simply.
- Hint at the transformation or result without giving everything away.
- Use a strong, directive CTA to guide the next action.
