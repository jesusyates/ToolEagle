## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling. I know you're doing it right now.

### Script beats
- Quick cut to you looking directly at the camera with a knowing smile.
- Show a super specific, universally awkward situation (like mishearing someone).
- Text overlay: 'We've all been here...'
- Cut to a humorous reenactment of the relatable moment.
- End with a question: 'Tell me I'm not the only one.'

### Caption / title
Relatable moments that make you say 'same'.

### CTA
Which one did you feel in your soul? Duet this and share your version.

### Hashtags
#relatable #fyp #viral #foryou #trending #comedy #storytime #funny #relatablecontent #tiktoktips #contentcreator #hookideas viraltiktok #creatorlife

### Why it works
- Triggers immediate recognition and a 'me too' response.
- Low barrier to engagement—easy to comment or duet.
- Builds a sense of community and shared experience.
