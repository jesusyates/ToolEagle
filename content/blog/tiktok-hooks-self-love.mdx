## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
If you struggle to look in the mirror, watch this.

### Script beats
- Show a 'before' mindset with a common struggle.
- Flip to the 'after' with a simple, actionable shift.
- Share a personal micro-story of applying this shift.
- End with a reflective question for the viewer.

### Caption / title
The self-love advice that actually works.

### CTA
Which one do you need to hear today?

### Hashtags
#selflove #selfcare #mentalhealth #personalgrowth #mindset #healing #selfworth #confidence #loveyourself #innerwork #growth #wellness #therapy #mindfulness

### Why it works
- Addresses a specific pain point immediately.
- Uses 'you' language for direct viewer connection.
- Promises a shift without being overly prescriptive.
- Leaves room for personal story and authenticity.
