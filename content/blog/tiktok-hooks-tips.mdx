## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I analyzed 1000 viral TikToks to find the exact hook patterns that work.

### Script beats
- Show the common problem or mistake visually in the first second.
- Quick cut to you demonstrating the solution or revealing the truth.
- On-screen text emphasizes the key benefit or surprising result.
- End with an open loop prompting a question in the comments.
