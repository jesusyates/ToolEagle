## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
This is what a $50,000 wedding cake actually looks like up close.

### Script beats
- Quick zoom on intricate cake detailing and texture.
- Cut to baker explaining one complex technique used.
- Wide shot showing the cake's full scale in venue.
- Text overlay revealing one surprising fact about cost.
- Satisfying final shot of a perfect slice being cut.

### Caption / title
We went inside a luxury bakery to see what goes into a five-figure wedding cake. The sugar flower work alone took over 80 hours to complete by hand.

### CTA
Save this for your dream wedding inspo and comment your cake flavor choice below!

### Hashtags
#WeddingTok #WeddingPlanning #BrideTips #WeddingCake #LuxuryWedding #WeddingVendor #CakeDecorating #WeddingInspo #BridalContent #TikTokWedding #WeddingDay #PlanningTips

### Why it works
- Uses high-dollar figure ('$50,000') to create immediate curiosity and perceived value.
- Focuses on a tangible, visual detail ('looks like up close') perfect for TikTok's visual platform.
- Taps into the aspirational and 'behind-the-scenes' interests of the wedding planning audience.
- Sets up a clear, satisfying visual payoff in the video content itself.
