## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
This yoga flow will fix your tight hips in under 60 seconds.

### Script beats
- Quick intro showing tight hip discomfort
- Demonstrate 3 specific hip-opening poses with clear cues
- Show before/after mobility difference
- Encourage viewers to try immediately
- End with question about their tightest area

### Caption / title
Tight hips from sitting all day? This quick yoga flow targets your hip flexors and glutes directly. I use these three poses daily to maintain mobility and prevent lower back pain.

### CTA
Comment 'HIPS' if you tried this flow and tell me which pose felt best! Save this for your next tight hip day.

### Hashtags
#YogaTikTok #YogaForBeginners #HipOpeningYoga #QuickYoga #YogaFlow #Flexibility #MobilityWork #YogaAtHome #TightHips #YogaPractice #MindBody #WellnessTok

### Why it works
- Targets a common pain point (tight hips from sitting) with immediate relevance
- Uses specific time frame (60 seconds) creating low barrier to entry
- Visual before/after demonstration provides clear value proof
- Action-oriented language encourages immediate trial and engagement
