## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get comments on your TikToks? Your title might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable pain point or curiosity gap.
- Deliver the promised value clearly in the first 3 seconds.
- Use quick cuts and on-screen text to emphasize key points.
- End with a question or prompt to encourage comments.
- Keep the entire video under 30 seconds for maximum impact.
