## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling for views on your gaming TikToks? Your title might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Show a quick clip of amazing gameplay with no context.
- Freeze frame and point to a specific element on screen.
- Ask a provocative question related to a common player frustration.
- Reveal your simple tip or secret in a text overlay.
- Show the result of using your advice with a satisfying clip.

### Caption / title
You're Building Your Starfield Ship Wrong (Here's Why)

### CTA
Which game should I break down next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#gamingtiktok #gamertips #gamingtips #videogames #gamingcontent #gamingcommunity #gamingnews #gamingclips #gamingvideos #gaminghelp #gamingstrategy #gamingguide #indiegames #pcgaming

### Why it works
- Targets a specific player pain point (inefficiency, confusion).
- Uses curiosity gap by promising a secret or correction.
- Leverages niche game keywords for the algorithm.
- Encourages comments through a direct question in the CTA.
