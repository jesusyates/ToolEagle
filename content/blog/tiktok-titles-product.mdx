## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling! I tested the viral product so you don't have to.

### Script beats
- Quick product reveal with a problem statement
- Demonstrate the key feature or result in an unexpected way
- Show a before and after or direct comparison
- Address a common doubt or objection head-on
- End with a clear value proposition or surprising benefit

### Caption / title
I put the viral [Product Name] to the test for 30 days. The results were not what I expected, especially for someone with [Your Specific Skin Type/Hair Type/Lifestyle Need]. Here's my completely honest review and whether it's worth your money.

### CTA
Comment 'TEST' if you want me to try another viral product next! Save this for your next shopping trip.

### Hashtags
#ProductReview #TikTokMadeMeBuyIt #AmazonFinds #CleanTok #SkincareRoutine #HomeOrganization #GadgetTok #Unboxing #BeforeAndAfter #HonestReview #MustHave #LifeHack #TryIt

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gap by hinting at unexpected results
- Establishes credibility with a specific testing timeframe
- Targets a niche audience with a concrete detail (e.g., skin type)
- Leverages social proof by acknowledging the product's viral status
- Creates relatability by focusing on a common problem or desire
