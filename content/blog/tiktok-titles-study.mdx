## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get saves on your study tips? Your title might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Show a common, frustrating study scenario (e.g., messy notes, no focus).
- Quickly tease the specific method or hack from the video.
- Visually demonstrate the 'after' – clean notes, a checked-off schedule.
- Mention the benefit (more time, less stress, higher grades).
- End with a call to save the video for their next study session.

### Caption / title
Save this for your next cram session! #StudyTok

### CTA
Which study struggle should I make a video about next? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#StudyTok #StudyTips #StudentLife #ExamSeason #Productivity #StudyMotivation #LearnOnTikTok #StudyHacks #AcademicTwitter #UniversityLife #StudyGram #Education #StudyWithMe

### Why it works
- Targets high-intent viewers actively searching for study solutions.
- Promises a specific, tangible outcome which encourages saves for later use.
- Uses curiosity gaps and problem-agitation to stop the scroll.
