## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get your team content seen? These title formulas are engineered for the TikTok algorithm.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable team pain point or desire.
- Show a specific, intriguing team habit or result.
- Hint at the valuable insight or method you'll reveal.
- Pose a direct question to your team-minded audience.
- End with a clear promise of a practical takeaway.

### Why it works
- Titles promise insider access to team dynamics, which is highly shareable.
- They use specific numbers and scenarios to feel concrete and credible.
- They tap into universal desires for better collaboration and efficiency.
