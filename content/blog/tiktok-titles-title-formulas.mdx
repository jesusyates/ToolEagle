## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop getting lost in the algorithm—use these title formulas to get seen.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable problem or desire.
- Show the unexpected method or twist.
- Demonstrate the transformation or result visually.
- Include a moment of genuine reaction or surprise.
- End with a clear takeaway or invitation.

### Caption / title
These title formulas are built for specific niches like DIY, dance, and ASMR to help you connect with your ideal audience. For example, a title targeting new dancers acknowledges the struggle of learning from sped-up videos.

### CTA
Which formula will you try first? Comment below with your niche and I'll suggest a tweak!

### Hashtags
#TikTokTips #ContentCreator #VideoMarketing #SocialMediaStrategy #TikTokGrowth #ContentStrategy #CreatorEconomy #DigitalMarketing #TikTokForBusiness #ViralTips #ContentCreation #SocialMediaTips

### Why it works
- Uses specific, searchable niche keywords that attract a targeted audience.
- Creates curiosity by presenting a problem-solution or challenge-outcome frame.
- Incorporates relatable scenarios (POV, 'trying') that encourage viewer identification.
- Avoids generic phrasing, making the content feel tailored and valuable.
