## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get views on your travel TikToks? Your title might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Show a quick clip of an amazing travel moment.
- Cut to a text overlay stating a common title mistake.
- Reveal a better title formula from the list.
- Show the same clip with the new, more engaging title text.
- End with a call to try more formulas for your own content.

### Caption / title
Stop using boring travel titles. Try these 50 formulas instead.

### CTA
Which title formula will you try first? Comment below!

- Titles promise specific value or solve a problem.

- Action-oriented language encourages viewers to watch and engage.
