## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get views on your tutorial Reels? Your title might be the problem.

### Script beats
- Show the frustrating 'before' result quickly.
- Demonstrate your key tip or trick clearly.
- Reveal the satisfying 'after' result with a transformation.
- On-screen text reinforces the key takeaway.
- End with a direct call to action.

### Caption / title
Save this for your next tutorial! Which title will you try first?

### CTA
Follow for more tips to grow your TikTok.

### Hashtags

### Why it works
- Titles promise a specific, achievable result quickly.
- They use active verbs and address a common pain point.
- The formulas are proven to drive curiosity and clicks.
- They are niche-specific but adaptable to many topics.
