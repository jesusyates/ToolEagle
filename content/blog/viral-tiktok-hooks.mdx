## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I asked 100 men what their biggest ick is. The number one answer surprised me.

### Script beats
- Open with a bold, curious statement about gathering niche data.
- Quickly list 2-3 humorous or relatable 'icks' to build engagement.
- Reveal the number one answer with a dramatic pause or text effect.
- Explain why this common behavior is perceived so negatively.
- Offer a simple, actionable alternative for the viewer.

### Caption / title
The #1 Ick According to 100 Men (It's Not What You Think)

### CTA
What's your biggest ick? Drop it below 👇

- Taps into universal curiosity about social norms and relationships.
- Uses a listicle format that promises a concrete, surprising reveal.
