## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get entries for your YouTube contest? These caption formulas are engineered for maximum conversion.

### Script beats
- Open with a high-energy prize reveal using on-screen graphics.
- Clearly state the three simple steps to enter in the first 30 seconds.
- Address common questions about eligibility and deadlines in the middle.
- Display the prize again in the final call-to-action with urgency.
- End with a pinned comment containing all official rules and links.

### Caption / title
YOU could win [PRIZE]! Here’s exactly how to enter our YouTube contest.

### CTA
Comment below with your entry before the deadline to be eligible!

### Hashtags
#YouTubeContest #GiveawayTime #WinWithMe #ContestAlert #YouTubePrize #FreeGiveaway #ContestEntry #YouTubeCommunity #SubscriberGiveaway #HowToWin #VideoContest #YouTubeTutorial #ContentCreator #EngagementTips

### Why it works
- Clear instructions reduce friction and increase entry completion rates.
- Social proof from comments and tags amplifies organic reach.
- Time-bound calls-to-action create urgency that drives immediate action.
- Community-focused prompts encourage interaction beyond the contest itself.
