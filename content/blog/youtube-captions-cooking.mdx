## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to write captions that make people watch your cooking Shorts?

### Script beats
- Show the delicious final result in the first second.
- Quickly list the simple ingredients or tools needed.
- Demonstrate the key technique or surprising twist.
- Show the satisfying serving or tasting moment.

### Caption / title
You won't believe how easy this restaurant-quality dish is.

### CTA
Save this recipe for your next easy dinner idea.

### Hashtags
#CookingShorts #EasyRecipes #FoodTutorial #HomeChef #QuickMeals #CookingHacks #RecipeIdeas #YouTubeShorts #FoodContent #LearnToCook #KitchenTips #Delicious

### Why it works
- Captions frame the 'problem' the recipe solves for the viewer.
- Action-oriented language encourages saves and shares.
- Niche hashtags help the right audience discover your content.
