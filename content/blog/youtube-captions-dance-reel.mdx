## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop posting dance reels with weak captions that get ignored.

### Script beats
- Open with a high-energy move on the very first beat.
- Showcase your best sequence in the middle three seconds.
- End with a sharp pose or a surprising transition to freeze frame.

### Caption / title
The footwork you didn't notice on the first watch.

### CTA
Duet this with your style and tag me!

### Hashtags
#DanceReel #Choreography #DanceTips #YouTubeShorts #DanceCommunity #LearnToDance #DanceVideo #Freestyle #DanceTutorial #DanceLife #DanceCover #ShortsDance

### Why it works
- Directly addresses the dancer's desire for recognition and growth.
- Provides clear, niche-specific templates that save time and mental energy.
- Encourages community interaction which boosts algorithmic visibility.
