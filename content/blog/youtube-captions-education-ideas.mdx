## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to make educational content click? Your captions might be the missing piece.

### Script beats
- Open with a question your audience secretly has.
- Visually demonstrate the core idea in under 15 seconds.
- Use on-screen text to reinforce the key learning point.
- End with a prompt that encourages application or comment.

### Caption / title
Your video teaches, but does your caption test?

### CTA
Pick one idea below and script your next short this week.

### Hashtags
#EduTok #LearnOnYouTube #StudyTube #EducationCreator #TeachingTips #EdTech #KnowledgeShare #ShortFormLearning #YouTubeEdu #CaptionHacks #CreatorEconomy #AcademicTwitter #VisualLearning #SkillBuilding

### Why it works
- Captions frame the learning objective before the video plays.
- Clear prompts reduce viewer confusion and increase completion rates.
- Searchable keywords in captions help the YouTube algorithm surface your content.
- A good caption turns passive viewers into active learners.
