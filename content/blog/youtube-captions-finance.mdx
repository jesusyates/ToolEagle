## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your bank doesn't want you to know this one money fact.

### Script beats
- Open with a surprising stat or contradictory statement.
- Show a quick visual of a bank statement or chart.
- Break down the 'why' in one clear, simple step.
- Contrast common advice with your actionable alternative.
- End with a direct question to prompt comments.

### Caption / title
Most people think building wealth is about saving more, but it's actually about optimizing the money you already have. This caption breaks down the one account fee you're probably overlooking that silently drains hundreds per year.

### CTA
Comment 'FEE' below if you've checked your statements for this, and save this Short to build your financial literacy toolkit.

### Hashtags
#FinanceTips #MoneyMindset #InvestingForBeginners #PersonalFinance #FinancialFreedom #WealthBuilding #StockMarket #SideHustle #Budgeting #DebtFree #FinancialLiteracy #YouTubeShorts #CreatorEconomy #MoneyHack

### Why it works
- Creates immediate curiosity and perceived secret knowledge.
- Addresses a universal pain point (losing money unknowingly).
- Offers a specific, niche action (checking for a fee).
- Uses strong command verbs ('Stop,' 'Start,' 'Build') for clarity.
