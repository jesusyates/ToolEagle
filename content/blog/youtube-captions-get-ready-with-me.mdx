## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to make your Get Ready With Me videos stand out? The right caption can be the difference between a scroll-past and a watch.

### Script beats
- Open with a relatable problem your routine solves.
- Showcase a unique product, technique, or time constraint.
- Share a personal reason or story behind the routine.
- Mention the final result or feeling viewers can expect.
- End with a question to prompt comments about their routines.

### Caption / title
Get Ready With Me: My 10-Minute Routine for Zoom Confidence

### CTA
What's the one product you can't get ready without? Tell me in the comments!

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #MakeupRoutine #SkincareRoutine #BeautyYouTube #MorningRoutine #MakeupTutorial #Routine #BeautyCreator #YouTubeTips #ContentCreator #WatchTime #YouTubeGrowth #BeautyCommunity

### Why it works
- Specific scenarios (e.g., 'for Zoom') attract a targeted audience.
- Promises a clear, time-bound benefit ('10-Minute', 'Confidence').
- Uses a familiar, searchable format that viewers actively seek.
