## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
You're wasting time on these 50 common problems. Stop it.

### Script beats
- Show a frustrating, common household problem.
- Quick cut to the simple hack solution in action.
- Show the satisfying end result and time saved.
- On-screen text reinforces the key steps.
- End with a prompt for the viewer's own hack.

### Caption / title
50 Life Hacks You'll Actually Use (Save This!)

### CTA
Which hack are you trying first? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#LifeHacks #YouTubeShorts #ShortFormContent #DIY #HomeHacks #ProductivityTips #LifeTips #CleaningHacks #Organization #SaveTime #EverydayHacks #QuickTips #GeniusHacks #HelpfulTips

### Why it works
- Hacks offer quick, perceived value with low effort.
- Visual 'before and after' is highly satisfying in short form.
- Listicles promise density of value, encouraging saves.
- Practical utility drives comments and personal sharing.
