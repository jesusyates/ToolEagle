## Review Captions

1. Use a generator to brainstorm multiple caption angles quickly.
2. Test different caption lengths to see what holds viewer attention.
3. Incorporate trending audio names directly into your caption text.
4. Ask a direct question in the caption to prompt immediate comments.
5. Place your most compelling hook in the first three caption lines.
6. Use emojis as visual bullet points to break up dense text.
7. Add a timestamp in the caption for a key moment in the video.
8. Create urgency with words like 'now' or 'before it's gone' in captions.
9. Pose a controversial opinion to spark debate in your comments section.
10. Use the caption to clarify complex points made in your short video.
11. Include a small error in the caption to encourage corrective comments.
12. Tease your next video topic within the current video's caption.
13. Rephrase your video's hook as a caption for reinforcement.
14. Add a relevant statistic or data point to boost caption credibility.
15. Use all caps sparingly for one or two powerful words in the caption.
16. Tell viewers to 'comment below' with a specific answer or result.
17. For tutorial shorts, list the steps in the caption for easy reference.
18. In captions, tag a relevant creator or source to encourage shares.
19. Use brackets like [WAIT FOR IT] to add suspense within your caption.
20. For comparison shorts, use the caption to label 'Before' and 'After'.
21. Include a surprising fact that complements your short video's content.
22. Prompt viewers to 'save this video' if they find the tip useful.
23. Use the caption to give credit for borrowed audio or video clips.
24. Add a location tag in the caption to attract a local audience.
25. For reaction shorts, use the caption to quote the original content.
26. Include a warning or disclaimer in the caption if necessary.
27. Use the caption to define a niche term used in your short video.
28. Prompt users to turn on notifications with a clear caption call.
29. Add a seasonal or holiday reference to make the caption timely.
30. Use the caption to link to a longer tutorial in your video description.
