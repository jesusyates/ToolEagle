## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop struggling with what to write under your skincare videos.

### Script beats
- Show a common skincare mistake in the first 3 seconds.
- Quickly demonstrate the correct technique or product application.
- On-screen text reinforces the key tip from the caption.
- End with a question to drive comments about routines.

### Caption / title
The way you apply your serum makes a huge difference.

### CTA
Which step do you always skip? Tell me in the comments.

### Hashtags
#skincaretips #skincareroutine #dermtips #beautytips #skincare101 #glowingskin #skincaretutorial #youtubeshorts #skincarelover #skincarecommunity #beautycreator #skincareshorts #skincarehacks

### Why it works
- Uses specific, niche terminology that skincare audiences recognize.
- Prompts engagement with a simple, relatable question in the CTA.
- Hashtags mix broad beauty tags with specific skincare communities.
