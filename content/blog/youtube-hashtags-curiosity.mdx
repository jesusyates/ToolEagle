### Hook
Struggling to get viewers to watch past the first 3 seconds? The secret isn't better edits—it's better curiosity triggers.

### Script beats
- Open with a question or visual that creates an immediate knowledge gap.
- Withhold just enough information to make the viewer need the answer.
- Promise a reveal that satisfies the itch you created early on.
- Use pacing and music to build anticipation toward your key point.
- End with a thought or question that lingers, prompting shares.
