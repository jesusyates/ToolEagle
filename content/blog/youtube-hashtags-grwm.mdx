## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using generic GRWM tags and start attracting your actual ideal viewer.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick, intriguing close-up of a unique product or step.
- Cut to a wider shot showing your environment and outfit vibe.
- Voiceover explains the specific 'why' behind today's routine.
- Show a satisfying transformation shot or the final polished look.
- End with a direct question to the viewer to spark comments.

### Caption / title
This isn't just another GRWM. This is my routine for getting ready as a vintage motorcycle mechanic apprentice. I focus on products that withstand grease and grime while still feeling like me.

### CTA
Comment below with the most unique job or event you've ever had to get ready for - I need ideas for my next video!

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #YouTubeShorts #NicheGRWM #RoutineReveal #BehindTheScenesBeauty #ASMRMakeup #SlowLivingRoutine #MinimalistGRWM #BudgetBeautyRoutine #SkincareGRWM #GRWMFashion #GRWMStorytime

### Why it works
- Niche details (like 'vintage mechanic apprentice') filter for highly interested viewers.
- Specific scenarios create relatable curiosity beyond just the beauty steps.
- Transformation focus provides satisfying before-and-after payoff for short attention spans.
- Direct CTA prompts community interaction, which signals engagement to the algorithm.
