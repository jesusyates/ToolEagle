## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get your travel videos seen? The right hashtags can change your reach.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick montage of stunning but low-view travel clips.
- Pose the question: 'Are you using generic tags that get lost?'
- Reveal the list as a solution for better niche targeting.
- Show a quick example of a video with and without good tags.
- End with a prompt to save the list for their next upload.

### Caption / title
Stop Using #Travel. Use These 50 YouTube Hashtags Instead.

### CTA
Save this list and try 5 new hashtags on your next short.

### Hashtags
#TravelYouTube #TravelCreator #Wanderlust #TravelTips #HiddenGems #AdventureTravel #CulturalTravel #BudgetTravel #LuxuryTravel #SoloTravel #TravelVlog #DestinationGuide #OffTheBeatenPath #TravelCommunity

### Why it works
- Moves beyond overly broad tags to attract engaged niche viewers.
- Combines location-specific and experience-based tags for dual reach.
- Aligns with YouTube's search and discovery system for travel content.
