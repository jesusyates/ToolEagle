## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop explaining and start showing with these walkthrough hashtags.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick visual of the finished result or problem
- Break the process into three clear, visual steps
- Show a common mistake and how to avoid it
- Demonstrate the final result with a satisfying reveal
- End with a prompt for viewers to try it themselves

### Caption / title
This walkthrough breaks down a complex task into simple, actionable steps. Perfect for visual learners who want to follow along in real time.

### CTA
Comment 'WALKTHROUGH' if you want a part two, and save this for your next project.

### Hashtags
#WalkthroughTutorial #StepByStepGuide #HowToYouTube #DIYProjects #LearnWithMe #TutorialShorts #ProcessVideo #EduTainment #SkillBuilding #VisualLearning #HowToHack #CreatorTips #ShortFormEdu

### Why it works
- Walkthroughs satisfy the 'how-to' intent behind many YouTube searches
- Breaking processes into steps increases watch time and completion rates
- Visual demonstrations reduce cognitive load compared to text explanations

## Best for YouTube
