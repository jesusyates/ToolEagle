## Promise hooks

1. "In this video, you'll learn exactly how to [result]"
2. "By the end of this, you'll have [tangible outcome]"
3. "I'm going to show you the [thing] that [achieved result]"
4. "This is the only [topic] guide you need"
5. "You're about to learn [skill] in [time]"
6. "I'll walk you through [process] step by step"
7. "This video will change how you [action]"
8. "Here's everything you need to know about [topic]"

## Story hooks

1. "Last week, something crazy happened…"
2. "I made a mistake that cost me [thing]. Here's what I learned."
3. "When I first started [thing], I had no idea…"
4. "This is the story of how I [achievement]"
5. "A year ago, I was [before]. Now I'm [after]."
6. "I tried [thing] for 30 days. The results surprised me."
7. "Nobody told me this when I started [thing]"
8. "The moment everything changed…"

## Question hooks

1. "What if I told you [surprising claim]?"
2. "Why does [common thing] actually work?"
3. "Have you ever wondered [curiosity]?"
4. "What's the one thing [experts] get wrong about [topic]?"
5. "Why do [group] struggle with [thing]?"
6. "What happens when you [action]?"
7. "Is [common belief] actually true?"
8. "What's the secret to [desired result]?"
