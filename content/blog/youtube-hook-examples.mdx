### Hook
Your first 30 seconds are a make-or-break moment. Let's dissect the hooks that work.

### Script beats
- State the common problem or misconception your viewer likely has.
- Introduce your unique perspective or contradictory data upfront.
- Tease the specific, actionable value you're about to deliver.
- Use quick cuts, on-screen text, or props to visualize the stakes.
- Pose a rhetorical question that transitions into your main content.
