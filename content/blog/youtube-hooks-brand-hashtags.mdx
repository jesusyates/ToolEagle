## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Most creators use brand hashtags wrong, and it's costing them partnerships.

### Script beats
- Reveal the common, ineffective approach to brand hashtags.
- Show the data or outcome from the strategic method.
- Break down the simple, actionable formula step-by-step.
- Preview the transformation for the viewer's own channel.

### Caption / title
The Brand Hashtag Hook That Grabs Attention

### CTA
Which brand will you target first with this? Comment below!

#BrandHashtagStrategy #YouTubeHooks #CreatorEconomy #BrandPartnerships #ContentStrategy #YouTubeMarketing #GrowYourChannel #SocialMediaTips #DigitalMarketing #InfluencerMarketing #SponsorshipSecrets #VideoMarketing

- Directly addresses the creator's desire for brand recognition.

- Frames the solution as a simple, replicable process.
