## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Your selfie captions are losing you viewers because they all sound the same.

### Script beats
- Show a selfie with a boring caption crossed out.
- Reveal the same selfie with a hook-driven caption.
- Break down why the new caption works psychologically.
- Show three quick examples in different niches.
- Challenge viewers to try one hook this week.

### Caption / title
Your Selfie Captions Are Boring (Here's How to Fix It)

### CTA
Which hook will you try first? Comment below!
