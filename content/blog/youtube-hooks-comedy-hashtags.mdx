## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Ever notice how we all have the same awkward internal monologue?

### Script beats
- Present the universal, relatable situation with a straight face.
- Exaggerate the internal thought process with quick cuts or text overlays.
- Pivot to your core point or punchline within the first 8 seconds.
- End on a visual gag or a 'we've all been there' look to the camera.

### Caption / title
The Shared Delusion of Being a Functional Adult

### CTA
Which one of these silent panics do you experience most? Comment below!

### Hashtags
#Comedy #Relatable #YouTubeShorts #Funny #Humor #SketchComedy #StandUp #DailyLife #FunnyVideos #ComedySkits #Sitcom #WebSeries #ShortFilm #ViralHooks

### Why it works
- Relatability is the core currency of viral comedy; these hooks tap into shared experiences.
- They frame ordinary frustration as a collective joke, creating an instant 'in-group' feeling.
- The specificity makes the scenario feel fresh despite being universally understood.
