## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I'm giving away $1000, but there's a catch you won't see coming.

### Script beats
- Show the prize visually in the first 3 seconds.
- State the simple, single action required to enter.
- Create false scarcity with a tight deadline.
- Hint at an unconventional selection method for the winner.
- End with a direct call to act immediately.

### Caption / title
You Won't Believe What I'm Giving Away (Seriously)

### CTA
Comment your answer below before the timer runs out to enter.

### Hashtags
#Contest #Giveaway #YouTube #Win #Prize #Free #Subscribers #Engagement #ContentCreator #Viral #HowToWin #YouTubeTips #Community

### Why it works
- Creates immediate perceived value for the viewer.
- Leverages curiosity and scarcity to drive quick action.
- Frames entry as simple, reducing friction to participate.
- Encourages comments which boosts the video's algorithmic ranking.
