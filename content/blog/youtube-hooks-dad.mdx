## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Dad content thrives on specific, nostalgic, and humorously observational hooks.

### Script beats
- Start with a hyper-specific dad behavior or stereotype.
- Present a curiosity gap with a 'why' or 'secret'.
- Use a personal anecdote or mini-story for relatability.
- Promise a reveal, explanation, or unexpected twist.
- End with a question or teaser that demands the video watch.
