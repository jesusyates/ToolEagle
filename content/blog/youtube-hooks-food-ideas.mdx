## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get viewers past the first three seconds? These food hooks are engineered for the YouTube algorithm.

### Script beats
- Open with a bold claim or surprising visual.
- Quickly demonstrate the problem or common mistake.
- Tease your unique solution or surprising result.
- Use tight cuts and text overlays to emphasize key points.
- End with a clear CTA before the viewer can click away.

### Caption / title

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gaps and 'secret' framing to promise immediate value.
- Addresses specific pain points (waste, failed recipes, cost) that food viewers have.
- Leverages trends and viral formats while adding a unique, niche-specific twist.
