## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop viewers from scrolling with humor that feels personal and instantly relatable.

### Script beats
- Open with the relatable, funny premise visually.
- Exaggerate the situation with your reaction or a quick cut.
- Pivot to your main point or punchline clearly.
- End on a high note or a call to engage.

### Caption / title
When your life is funnier than a sitcom. #relatable #funny

### CTA
Which one made you laugh? Tell me in the comments!

### Hashtags
#funny #comedy #relatable #youtubehooks #contentcreator #viralhooks #funnyshorts #humor #storytime #dailyvlog #comedyvideos #fyp #shorts

### Why it works
- Humor lowers barriers and builds quick connection.
- Relatable scenarios trigger the 'that's so me' reaction.
- Short, punchy setups match YouTube Shorts' fast pace.
- Emotional payoff (laughter) encourages shares and repeats.
