## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
I'm doing my makeup in 10 minutes before my flight boards—watch the chaos.

### Script beats
- Open with a time constraint or unique scenario.
- Quickly show the key product or technique causing drama.
- Tease the satisfying transformation or funny outcome.
- Ask a question to engage viewers in the comments.

### Caption / title
My 10-Minute Airport Makeup Routine Before Boarding

### CTA
What's the wildest place you've ever done your makeup? Tell me below!

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #MakeupRoutine #BeautyYouTube #MakeupTutorial #SkincareRoutine #BeautyCreator #MakeupChallenge #EverydayMakeup #QuickMakeup #MakeupForBeginners #ViralMakeup #BeautyHacks

### Why it works
- Creates immediate intrigue with a specific, relatable time pressure.
- Promises a visual journey and transformation in a short video format.
- Uses a common viewer pain point (getting ready fast) as the core premise.
