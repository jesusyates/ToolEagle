## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop scrolling! I just got the new [Product Name] and the results are shocking.

### Script beats
- Quick cut of the most visually impressive feature in action.
- Close-up on my genuine reaction (surprise, confusion, joy).
- Cut to a clear side-by-side comparison with the old version.
- Quick montage of the top 3 benefits in under 10 seconds.
- End on a lingering shot of the product with a question for viewers.

### Caption / title
Testing The HYPED New [Product Category] - Better Than Expected?

### CTA
Would you buy this? Let me know in the comments below!

### Hashtags
#NewArrival #Unboxing #ProductReview #FirstImpressions #Tech #Gadgets #YouTubeShorts #ContentCreator #AmazonFinds #Drop #LatestTech #TryOnHaul #Review #Unbox

### Why it works
- Uses curiosity gap by hinting at 'shocking' results.
- Visual-first beats cater to short attention spans.
- Asks a direct, low-effort question to prompt comments.
