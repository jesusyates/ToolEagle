## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
This $5 kitchen gadget just replaced my entire knife set.

### Script beats
- Quick close-up of the gadget in action
- Side-by-side comparison with traditional knives
- Demonstration of a difficult cutting task made easy
- Reaction shot of genuine surprise
- Clear shot of the price point on screen

### Caption / title
I was skeptical about this ultra-affordable kitchen tool, but it genuinely outperformed my expensive professional knife set for daily vegetable prep. The ergonomic handle and ceramic blade made dicing onions and mincing garlic effortless without needing constant sharpening.

### CTA
Comment 'GADGET' below if you want the exact Amazon link, and save this Short to find it later when you're upgrading your kitchen!

### Hashtags
#KitchenGadgets #AmazonFinds #CookingTools #HomeCook #MealPrep #BudgetFriendly #KitchenHacks #TimeSaver #ProductReview #MustHave #ShortFormReview #YouTubeShorts

### Why it works
- Uses a specific, relatable price point ('$5') to create immediate intrigue and value perception.
- Makes a bold, benefit-driven claim ('replaced my entire set') that promises a significant outcome.
- Targets a common pain point (needing multiple knives/clutter) with a simple solution.
- Works for the YouTube Shorts format by being visual, quick to demo, and generating 'how does that work?' curiosity.
