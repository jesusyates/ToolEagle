## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop viewers from scrolling past your review with these proven hooks.

### Script beats
- State the product or category clearly upfront.
- Introduce a strong angle: surprise, regret, comparison, or result.
- Hint at the key insight or verdict without giving it all away.
- Pose a question or highlight a contradiction the review will solve.
- Use specific numbers, prices, or timeframes for credibility.

### Caption / title
YouTube Hooks That Make Your Reviews Unskippable

### CTA

### Why it works
- Creates immediate curiosity about a specific outcome or verdict.
- Uses concrete details (price, time, results) to feel authentic.
- Targets viewer pain points or desires directly in the first line.
- Frames the review as a story or revelation, not just a list.
