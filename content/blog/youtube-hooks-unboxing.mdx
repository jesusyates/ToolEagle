## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
This unboxing hook formula focuses on unexpected outcomes and packaging details.

### Script beats
- Show the external packaging briefly
- Build anticipation with commentary
- Reveal the product with a clear reaction
- Highlight one surprising detail
- End with a question or tease

### Caption / title
The packaging was more exciting than the product inside.

### CTA
