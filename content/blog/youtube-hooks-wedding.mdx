## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
We spent $500 on wedding favors that nobody actually took home.

### Script beats
- Quick shot of beautifully wrapped favors on a table.
- Cut to empty table at end of night with a few stragglers.
- Close-up on creator's face with a 'I told you so' look.
- Pan over to a huge pile of leftover favors in a box.
- Text overlay: 'Save your money for the pizza truck instead.'

### Caption / title
This is what happens when you overthink your wedding favors. We went with custom koozies, thinking they'd be a hit, but at the end of the night, we had 150 left. Learn from our mistake and invest in experiences, not trinkets.

### CTA
Comment below with the worst wedding favor you've ever received, and save this for your future planning!

### Hashtags
#WeddingFail #WeddingPlanning #BrideTips #GroomLife #DIYWedding #WeddingVendor #WeddingContent #WeddingTok #Marriage #WeddingAdvice #WeddingDay #BridalParty #WeddingInspo #SayYesToTheDress

### Why it works
- Uses a specific, relatable financial mistake to create instant curiosity.
- Taps into the universal wedding experience of wasted budget and planning stress.
- Visual beats are simple to film and create a clear, humorous story arc.
- The CTA prompts easy engagement by asking for similar stories.
