## The hook (0-30 seconds)

Your first 30 seconds decide if viewers stay. Include:

1. **Promise** — What they'll learn or get
2. **Curiosity** — Why they need to keep watching
3. **Credibility** — Why you're the one to teach this

Example: "In this video, you'll learn the exact script structure I use for every video. By the end, you'll have a template you can copy."

## The main content

Break your topic into 3-5 clear sections:

1. **Intro to each section** — "First, let's talk about…"
2. **Explanation** — The meat of the content
3. **Example** — Make it concrete
4. **Transition** — "Now that we've covered X, let's look at Y"

## The outro and CTA

1. **Recap** — "So to summarize…"
2. **Next step** — "Your homework is…"
3. **Subscribe CTA** — "If this helped, hit subscribe"
4. **Comment CTA** — "Drop your biggest takeaway below"
