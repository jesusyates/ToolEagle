## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling for views? Your title might be the culprit. Let's unpack a battle-tested social media content package built around a proven title formula.

### Script beats
- Open with a quick, relatable pain point about low click-through rates.
- Introduce the core title formula: 'The [Number] [Adjective] Ways to [Verb] [Noun]'.
- Break down each component: why the number works, how to choose the right adjective, and selecting a high-interest verb-noun combo.
- Show a rapid-fire montage of 3-4 different niche applications of the same formula.
- Transition to the CTA by emphasizing that this is just one of many patterns.
