## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop wondering why your business videos aren't getting views.

### Script beats
- Open with a specific, painful problem your target business owner faces.
- Quickly tease the core solution or framework your video provides.
- Show a brief, authentic glimpse of the result (e.g., a graph, dashboard, or testimonial snippet).
- Pose a direct question that makes the viewer assess their own situation.
- End on the value of implementing just one idea from the video.

### Caption / title
Most business content fails to connect because the titles are too generic. For a consulting firm targeting e-commerce brands, a title like 'How we helped a DTC skincare brand reduce customer acquisition cost by 30% in Q3' performs better because it specifies the niche, the metric, and the timeframe.

### CTA
Comment below with one metric you want to improve this quarter, and we'll suggest a video idea.

### Hashtags
#BusinessTips #EntrepreneurLife #StartupGrowth #MarketingStrategy #SmallBusinessTips #BusinessHacks #LeadershipTips #ProductivityHacks #SideHustleIdeas #DigitalMarketing #AgencyLife #SaaSMarketing #BusinessConsultant #WorkflowAutomation

### Why it works
- Uses specific numbers, timeframes, and niche details to build credibility and curiosity.
- Focuses on a clear transformation (saving time, increasing revenue, reducing cost) that appeals directly to business pain points.
- The 'how we' or 'our' framing implies proven, real-world experience rather than theoretical advice.
- Poses a question or makes a claim that prompts the viewer to self-reflect on their own business gaps.
