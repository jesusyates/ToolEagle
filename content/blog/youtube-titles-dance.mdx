## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get clicks on your dance tutorials? Your title is the first move your audience sees.

### Script beats
- Open with the common frustration of great content getting low views.
- Introduce the concept of title formulas as a strategic tool, not a guarantee.
- Present the list as 50 tested starting points to adapt.
- Emphasize pairing a strong title with valuable content inside the video.

### Caption / title
50 YouTube Titles for Dance Creators to Beat the Algorithm

### CTA
Pick 3 titles, adapt them for your next video, and see what resonates.

### Hashtags
#DanceTutorial #YouTubeTips #ContentCreator #DanceLife #Choreography #VideoMarketing #DanceCoach #Tutorial #ContentStrategy #DigitalCreator #DanceCommunity #ArtistTips

### Why it works
- Titles use specific dance genres and skill levels to attract a targeted viewer.
- Formulas incorporate curiosity ('secret', 'guide', 'how to') and immediacy ('this weekend', 'under ten minutes').
- Action-oriented language ('Learn', 'Break down', 'Improve') promises clear value to the viewer.
