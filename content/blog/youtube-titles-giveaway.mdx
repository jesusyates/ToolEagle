## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to get people to enter your YouTube giveaway? The right title makes all the difference.

### Script beats
- Open with the problem of low giveaway engagement.
- Introduce the list of 50 proven title formulas.
- Explain how to adapt a formula for your niche.
- Show a quick example of customizing a title.
- Prompt them to pick one and test it now.

### Caption / title
50 YouTube Giveaway Titles That Actually Get Clicks
