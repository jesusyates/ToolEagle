## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Stop using generic 'GRWM' titles. Here are 50 scroll-stopping alternatives.

### Script beats
- Hook with a specific scenario or emotional goal
- Show the key products or steps that are unique to this routine
- Share a personal tip or struggle related to the situation
- Quickly demonstrate a key technique or application
- End with the transformed feeling or ready-to-go look

### Caption / title
These GRWM titles work because they promise a specific transformation tied to a relatable life event. Instead of just 'GRWM', they hint at a story, a solution, or a feeling the viewer is seeking.

### CTA
Which of these life moments should I film a full GRWM for next? Comment your vote and save this list for your next video idea.

### Hashtags
#GRWM #GetReadyWithMe #MorningRoutine #MakeupRoutine #BeautyRoutine #YouTubeShorts #RoutineVlog #LifeHacks #BeautyTips #MakeupTutorial #ShortForm #ContentTips #CreatorToolkit

### Why it works
- Targets search intent for specific situations (e.g., 'first date makeup')
- Uses curiosity gap by mentioning a specific goal or challenge
- Inherently relatable, tapping into shared life experiences
- More descriptive than 'GRWM', improving click-through rate
