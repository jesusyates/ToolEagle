## Publish-ready package framing (what to paste)

### Hook
Struggling to make your mystery videos stand out? The right title is your first clue.

### Script beats
- Open with a question that highlights an information gap.
- Present the mystery with specific, tantalizing details.
- Hint at a revelation or twist without giving it all away.
- Frame the payoff for the viewer's curiosity.
- End with a direct call to engage with the content.
