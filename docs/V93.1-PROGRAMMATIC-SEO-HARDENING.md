## Routes / APIs

| Area | Change |
|------|--------|
| **EN pseo** | `/pseo/...` — metadata, `noindex` when not indexable, JSON-LD (Breadcrumb + FAQ / HowTo) |

| **GET `/api/sitemap/generate`** | JSON manifest uses indexable totals |

## Indexability (sitemap + robots)

Default floors (`DEFAULT_INDEX_THRESHOLDS` in `src/lib/programmatic-seo.ts`):

- `revenue_score >= 0.01`
- `quality_score >= 0.25`
- `review_status === 'approved'`
- `is_blacklisted === false`

---

## Skipped generation reasons

`blacklisted` | `rejected` | `pending_review` | `review_status_mismatch` | `low_revenue_score` | `low_quality_score` | `bad_slug` | `bad_keyword` | `locale_mismatch`
